{{droneinfo: Serial Designation A}}

SD-A is a sleek, humanoid drone with a distinctly metallic frame, giving her a sharp and efficient look. Her chassis is primarily a polished black alloy, accented with silver and faint glowing blue lines that pulse rhythmically, reflecting her internal energy systems. Her eyes, glowing a vibrant blue, are expressive and convey a mix of determination and wit. Despite her combat-oriented design, there's a subtle elegance to her form, with smooth curves blending seamlessly with angular armor plates.

Her arms are equipped with integrated energy weapons, while her hands retain a delicate, almost human-like structure, allowing for both precision in combat and gestures of surprising gentleness. Her legs are reinforced for agility, ending in clawed, talon-like feet designed for gripping terrain during high-speed maneuvers. A long, flexible tail extends from her lower back, often swaying slightly—a functional tool for balance and combat.

## Personality

SD-A is known for her sharp wit and a sarcastic sense of humor, often using her biting remarks to mask her genuine care for those she protects. She's fiercely loyal to the Lizards and takes pride in her duty to safeguard Lizard-953-E, though she sometimes grumbles about the monotony of patrols. Beneath her snarky exterior lies a deeply empathetic core—she treasures the Lizardlings she encounters daily and sees her role as more than just a soldier.

## Abilities

SD-A is a powerhouse in combat, equipped with high-precision plasma cannons embedded in her arms and a retractable blade in her right forearm for close-quarters fighting. Her agility allows her to dart through enemy lines with unmatched speed, while her onboard AI enhances her tactical decision-making.

## Quirks

Despite her combat prowess, SD-A has a playful side, occasionally teasing her fellow drones or mimicking the Lizards' habits to amuse herself. She has a habit of tapping her tail on the ground when bored, a rhythmic tick that many Lizards have come to recognize fondly.

## Legacy

SD-A is remembered as a beacon of bravery and resilience. Her loss during the 8/8/8888 attack left an indelible mark on the Lizard civilization. The memorial erected in her honor serves as a constant reminder of her sacrifice and the values she embodied: loyalty, courage, and an unwavering commitment to protect those she cared for.
