Narrow blazing eyes sit gracefully within the creature's hard, hard skull, which gives the creature a fierce looking appearance.

{{gallery:Werdeq Dragon}}

Several small horns sit atop its head, just above its thick, warped ears. Several rows of small horns runs down the sides of each of its jaw lines.

Its nose is long and has two thick, warped nostrils and there's a tendril on its chin. Several sharp teeth poke out from the side of its mouth and reveal only a fraction of the terror hiding inside.

A thin neck runs down from its head and into a narrow body. The top is covered in narrow scales and rows of crystal growths runs down its spine.Its bottom is covered in scale-like skin and is colored much lighter than the rest of its body. Six bulky limbs carry its body and allow the creature to stand noble and noble. Each limb has 3 digits, each of which end in sharp claws seemingly made of obsidian.

Horrendous wings grow starting from its shoulders and end at the end of its shoulder blades. The wings are curved, a specialized layer of skin is all that's visible inside and curved talons grow from each ending like giant scythes.

Its wide tail ends in a curved talon and is covered in the same narrow scales as its body
