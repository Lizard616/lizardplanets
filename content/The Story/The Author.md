{{droneinfo:Bryce}}

Soooooo… I was born in Tampa, Florida, a city known for its sunny weather, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural history. Growing up, I developed a love for sci-fi, action, and horror genres, which naturally led me to become a huge fan of *Murder Drones*, *Independence Day*, and *Doom*. 

*Murder Drones* drew me in with its unique blend of dark humor, intense action, and the complex personalities of the disassembly drones. 

*Independence Day* amazed me with its epic alien invasion storyline, larger-than-life battles, and that unforgettable 'We will not go quietly into the night' speech. 

As for *Doom*, its fast-paced gameplay, brutal combat, and legendary soundtrack had me hooked from the start. These franchises have fueled my creativity and inspired me in many of the stories, concepts, and characters I've created over time.
