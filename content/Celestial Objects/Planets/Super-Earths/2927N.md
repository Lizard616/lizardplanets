**2927N** is a massive terrestrial exoplanet discovered by the Lizards in the outer regions of the Kertom Expanse, approximately **315 light-years** from Lizard-953-E. The planet orbits a **K-type orange dwarf**, designated **Kertom-2927**, at a distance of **0.84 AU**, placing it just inside the inner edge of the system's habitable zone.

With a diameter of **17,400 km** and a mass of **2.75 Earth masses**, 2927N boasts a surface gravity of **1.36 g**, giving it a dense atmosphere rich in **oxygen (28%)**, **nitrogen (65%)**, and trace amounts of **argon** and **xenon**, making it technically breathable for most Lizard species with minor adaptation. However, unusually high concentrations of **ozone (3%)** and naturally occurring airborne metallic particulates pose health risks without filtration.

The planet's surface is dominated by **dark basaltic continents** streaked with veins of luminescent minerals, a phenomenon caused by ancient tectonic activity and high radiation from the system's active stellar wind. These minerals, primarily **Zytherium compounds**, are sought after by the Lizards for advanced energy storage applications.

The climate of 2927N is harsh — averaging **40°C (104°F)** on equatorial plains with severe electromagnetic storms sweeping across the planet every few weeks due to its magnetosphere's frequent collapses and reformations. Despite this, **sturdy xerophytic flora** and **extremophile fauna** thrive, especially in polar regions where temperatures drop to a survivable **-10°C (14°F)**.

The Lizards have established **mining outposts and shielded research colonies** on 2927N's southern continent, operating under the protection of orbital defense platforms. Politically, the planet is governed as a **direct resource colony** under the Lizardian Ministry of Extraction, with strict off-world export controls due to the strategic value of Holmium.
