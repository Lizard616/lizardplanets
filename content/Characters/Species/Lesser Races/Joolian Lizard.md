This odd, but common creature is a type of reptile. It's about the size of a gecko has and a long, useless tail.

{{gallery:Joolian Lizard}}

They have a thin, rough skin covered in small, strong scales, which is usually either green, light green or light brown or a combination of these colors.

They live in hot areas and are relatively rare. They're herbivores and their large, long mouths, their teeth and wide tongue are ideal for eating berries.They're nocturnal and rely on their extra sense and hearing to get around. They do have large, elliptic eyes, but their sight is lacking.

They have narrow noses and small, round ears. Their heads are relatively small in comparison to their bodies.

They make sounds ranging from relatively high pitched to very low pitched and have a extremely large range of sounds they make to indicate discoveries, dangers and otherwise communicate with each other.

These creatures are fairly violent, but they tend to let their territory be taken be stronger creatures. They mate three times a year and they mate with a select group of partners for life. Which, with their long lifepans, is quite common among other species as well.
