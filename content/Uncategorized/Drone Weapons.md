## Weapons Equipped on Disassembly Drones
Disassembly drones are equipped with a versatile array of weapons that can be seamlessly switched out for standard hands, allowing for adaptability in both combat and utility scenarios. Each weapon is designed to enhance the drone's capabilities while retaining functionality for manual tasks.

### EMP Emitter

The **EMP Emitter** can be deployed in place of a standard hand, allowing the drone to disable electronic systems within a specified radius. Its compact barrel design features a charging mechanism for multiple uses and a targeting interface for precise aiming, ensuring the drone can neutralize threats without direct confrontation.

{{gallery:Drone Weapons}}

### Retractable Sword

When combat is imminent, the drone can replace a hand with the **Retractable Sword**. This melee weapon extends rapidly, revealing a blade made of ultra-durable alloys. The sword features a locking mechanism to secure it during use and an energy conduit that enhances its cutting power through heat generation, making it effective for close-quarters engagements.

### Rocket Launcher

The **Rocket Launcher** can be mounted in place of one of the drone's hands, resembling a compact missile pod. It fires guided or unguided rockets with explosive, incendiary, or specialized payloads. Equipped with a digital targeting system, the launcher allows for precise shots, enabling the drone to engage targets effectively from a distance.

### Claws

The **Claws** can be utilized in lieu of standard hands, providing both offensive capabilities and utility functions. Made from reinforced alloys, these retractable claws can slice through enemies and grip objects for manipulation. They enhance the drone's agility and ability to traverse diverse terrains.

### Plasma Cannon

The **Plasma Cannon** can replace one of the drone's hands, firing concentrated bolts of superheated plasma. This weapon is essential for melting through armor and inflicting substantial damage on various targets. The barrel design includes cooling vents to manage heat buildup, ensuring sustained fire during prolonged engagements.

### Personal Force Field Generator

Integrated into the drone's design, the **Personal Force Field Generator** can be activated while the drone uses either its hands or other weapons. It projects a protective energy barrier around the drone, capable of deflecting projectiles and absorbing energy-based attacks, providing essential defense in combat situations.

### Yabitanite Injector

The **Yabitanite Injector** takes the form of a compact MP5 rifle, allowing the drone to switch its hand for targeted disassembly operations. It features a trigger mechanism for controlled release, a modular barrel for dispersal patterns, and an integrated scope for precise aiming, making it ideal for breaking down fortified structures.

### Yabitanite Tail

The **Yabitanite Tail** extends from the drone's rear and can be deployed while maintaining standard hands. The tail can deliver concentrated sprays or solid projectiles of Yabitanite at high velocity, allowing for both offensive strikes and defensive maneuvers, enhancing the drone's versatility in combat.

### Yabitanite Headband

The **Yabitanite Headband** can be worn while the drone operates with its standard hands or any weapon. It enhances the drone's control over Yabitanite, enabling real-time manipulation of the substance. This headband can emit localized waves of Yabitanite to disrupt nearby enemies and interfere with their systems.

### Quantum Railgun

The **Quantum Railgun** can replace one hand, using electromagnetic forces to launch projectiles at near-light speeds. Its sleek design includes a stabilization system for accuracy and an advanced targeting computer, making it effective for long-range engagements against heavily armored targets.

### MP5 Rifle

The **MP5 Rifle** can easily substitute for a standard hand, providing rapid-fire capabilities with customizable ammunition. Its lightweight design allows for quick handling, ensuring the drone can engage in combat while still performing manual tasks when necessary.

### 10,000,000,000 Watt Laser

The **10,000,000,000 Watt Laser** can be mounted in place of a hand, providing overwhelming destructive power. Capable of instantaneously vaporizing targets, this massive laser system includes advanced cooling and targeting mechanisms, ensuring sustained fire and accuracy over long distances.
