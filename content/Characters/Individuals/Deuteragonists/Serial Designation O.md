{{character:O}}

Serial Designation O is a marvel of engineering and a terrifying enigma among the Lizard civilization's network of disassembly drones. This drone, known simply as "O" to those few who are aware of its existence, is whispered about in hushed tones. Its presence is more of a myth than a confirmed reality, a shadowy figure spoken of only in rumors and distant sightings on the densely populated, city-covered planet Lizard-753-Y. Unlike other drones, O's design emphasizes stealth, versatility, and lethality in urban environments, making it a perfect predator within the concrete and neon maze of this advanced city-world.

At first glance, Serial Designation O is humanoid in shape but larger, with an imposing stature that hints at an unnaturally agile and powerful frame. Standing at around 8 feet tall, O's structure is streamlined and devoid of any unnecessary components, crafted to minimize air resistance and eliminate sound during movement. Its limbs are long and sinuous, designed for rapid, fluid movement across all kinds of surfaces. Each segment of its arms and legs is encased in a complex exoskeleton made from a highly durable, matte-black composite material that absorbs light rather than reflecting it, giving O a spectral presence. When in motion, O is almost entirely invisible, blending seamlessly into shadows and vanishing in low-light conditions. Its silhouette is barely detectable, except for those with keen eyes or advanced sensors—and even then, O's advanced cloaking technology makes it one of the hardest drones to spot in the entire Lizard military arsenal.

{{gallery:Serial Designation O}}

O's head is relatively small and angular, with a streamlined, helmet-like shape that enhances its aerodynamic profile. Instead of the usual optics seen on other drones, O's "eyes" are a pair of deep, electric blue lights embedded within a narrow visor slit, giving it an intense, penetrating stare. These blue lights are equipped with variable brightness and can dim to nearly nothing, making it appear as though O's "eyes" vanish in complete darkness. This visual design allows O to intimidate its targets by simply adjusting the intensity of its gaze; at its brightest, O's electric blue eyes can be seen even from a distance, cutting through fog and smoke like a pair of spectral headlights. Despite this eerie luminescence, O can deactivate this feature to blend seamlessly into its environment.

One of O's most distinguishing characteristics is its ultra-silent movement capability. The engineers of the Lizard civilization fitted O with precision-engineered joints that operate without a hint of noise. Unlike standard drones that emit faint mechanical whirring sounds when they move, O's movement is akin to that of a predatory feline—silent, calculated, and fluid. This silence makes O perfect for urban environments, where sound can easily alert targets. Whether scaling the side of a skyscraper, slipping through narrow alleyways, or moving across rooftops, O leaves no audible trace of its presence, instilling an eerie sense of dread in those who know it may be lurking nearby.

O's armor plating is a feat of modern Lizard engineering. It is forged from a proprietary composite material that provides exceptional durability while remaining lightweight, allowing for unmatched agility. The outer layer of the armor is coated in a special material that not only dampens sound but also resists most energy and ballistic attacks. This armor is composed of layered segments that shift smoothly as O moves, ensuring no restriction in flexibility. The matte black finish of O's plating includes flecks of dark green and silvery hues, giving the armor an almost organic, scale-like appearance when viewed up close—a design choice perhaps inspired by the Lizard civilization itself. Embedded within the armor are subtle channels that can release a cloud of particulate matter to further cloak O, rendering it nearly undetectable even by advanced sensors.

One of O's most feared offensive features is the Yabitanite injector embedded in its left arm. This weapon system is unique to O and makes use of Yabitanite, a rare and deadly substance developed by the Lizards. Yabitanite has an atomic structure that disrupts matter on a fundamental level, tearing it apart into quarks by destabilizing atomic bonds. O's left arm houses a pressurized reservoir of Yabitanite in a liquid form, which is dispensed via specialized nozzles. When activated, O can spray this substance as a thin, mist-like vapor or project it in a concentrated stream, allowing it to dissolve anything it comes into contact with. The injector system is designed with extreme precision, able to deploy Yabitanite in measured amounts, whether to create a quick barrier of corrosive mist or to deliver a devastatingly targeted attack. This adaptability in its weaponry makes O lethal in close quarters, as even a single drop of Yabitanite can dissolve metal, flesh, and nearly any known material.

In addition to the Yabitanite injector, O is equipped with a sleek, syringe-like tail that extends from the base of its spine. Unlike retractable tails, O's tail is a permanent fixture, streamlined against its back when not in use but still ready to strike at any moment. The tail is segmented and constructed from the same dark, matte material as the rest of O's armor, with a needle-like injector at the tip designed for precise Yabitanite delivery directly into targets. This tail can extend several feet beyond O's body, allowing it to pierce through armor and deliver a fatal injection even when the target is out of arm's reach. The tail moves with eerie precision and speed, making it a highly effective tool for assassination and close combat. In battle, O's tail can lash out like a whip, injecting Yabitanite into opponents before they even realize they've been hit.

O's artificial intelligence is another point of intrigue. Unlike standard disassembly drones, O has a high level of autonomy and situational awareness, equipped with a sophisticated decision-making system that allows it to act independently when disconnected from central command. This AI is known to be particularly ruthless and efficient, designed to prioritize the protection of Lizard-753-Y at all costs. O's programming incorporates advanced learning algorithms, allowing it to analyze enemy tactics, predict movements, and adapt its own strategies accordingly. Over time, O has become known not just as a weapon but as a highly skilled hunter, able to anticipate its targets' moves and cut them down with chilling efficiency.

O's movement capabilities are further enhanced by an advanced agility system. Its feet are equipped with retractable magnetic claws, allowing it to cling to metallic surfaces and scale buildings as if gravity were a mere suggestion. These claws can extend or retract depending on the surface, enabling O to traverse both metal and glass structures with ease. The claws are sharp enough to pierce even the hardest materials, ensuring that O can latch onto almost any surface, whether it's scaling the side of a high-rise or hanging upside down from an overpass. This versatility allows O to navigate Lizard-753-Y's sprawling urban landscape, adapting to its vertical nature and using the city itself as both a weapon and a hiding place.

O's sensor array is finely tuned to pick up the slightest of movements and sounds in its environment. Its head houses multiple sensors that can scan a wide range of wavelengths, from infrared to ultraviolet, allowing it to detect hidden targets even through walls or in complete darkness. O can track heat signatures, listen to the faintest sounds, and monitor electromagnetic activity, making it virtually impossible for targets to escape its notice once they're within range. This sensor suite also includes an advanced cloaking detection algorithm that enables O to identify cloaked or stealth-enabled enemies, neutralizing one of the few defenses that would otherwise render it vulnerable.

In conclusion, Serial Designation O is a silent, methodical, and virtually unstoppable enforcer of the Lizard civilization, engineered with unparalleled stealth, adaptability, and lethal precision. Its presence alone strikes fear into those who know of its legend, and even the most advanced invaders know that setting foot on Lizard-753-Y risks encountering this spectral drone, lurking in the shadows, waiting patiently to carry out its mission with unflinching resolve.
