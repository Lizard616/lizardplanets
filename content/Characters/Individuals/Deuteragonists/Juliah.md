**Juliah Jeknob**, more commonly referred to as **Juliah**, is the main Tritagonist of Lizard-Planets.

She is the rebellious daughter of [Jeremiah](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeremiah) and [Dora](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dora) and initially sought to battle against [Murder Drones](/characters/species/main-races/murder-drones/), who accidentally hunt her fellow [Workers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Workers), as well as Arynn and [People (Not Humans)](/characters/species/main-races/people-not-humans/) as a whole. Eventually, she grew a friendship with [Y](/characters/individuals/deuteragonists/serial-designation-y/) but the two discovered the [K̵̛̲̬͍̦̉̉̚͝ͅð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Absolute_solver), an immensely powerful artificial intelligence that afflicts her.

Juliah succeeded in destroying [K̵̛̲̬͍̦̉̉̚͝ͅð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗](/characters/individuals/tetragonists/k-knk-a/)'s heart, preventing the ð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗ from taking back her form by swallowing her singularity core of the Konkua. This action caused the melded form of A Lizard's corpse and K̵̛̲̬͍̦̉̉̚͝ͅð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗'s drone body to collapse into oil and blood, but it inadvertently made Juliah the K̵̛̲̬͍̦̉̉̚͝ͅð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗'s new host, which let her inherit the abilities of her late predecessor.

## Physical Appearance
Juliah takes on the appearance of a short [Worker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Worker) with neon purple eyes and short, dull purple hair. She wears a black striped beanie with a glittery bobble at the tip, and black boots with long, dark purple light purple striped socks. She also wears a black hoodie with a white emblem on the front depicting a battery with two bones going across it, creating an "X" shape. The bottom of the hoodie has two white stripes wrapping around it, and the left sleeve has a white radioactive symbol on it. Beneath her hoodie is a short, frilly, black skirt. She also has a black choker around her neck with a skull and the number "002" on it.

## Personality
Juliah acts quite rebellious and believes that all of her fellow [Worker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Worker)<nowiki/>s should rise and stand against their [creators](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jcjenson) who wish to have them all dealt with like garbage, much to the general unconcerned nature of her peers. She has been described as a "little devious twerp."

Juliah perceives herself as an "angsty rebellious teen," and notes that she has "crippling daddy issues," implying that her strained relationship with [her father](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Khan_doorman) may have led to her nature and "repressed emotional baggage." She can be obstinate at times, insisting on her ideals and actions even if others disagree with her, although this may more directly be a result of her lack of caring in social norms and potentially sociopathic traits making her lack empathy for her peers.

However, following her self-exile from her colony, she begins to become paranoid with the arrival of a [bizarre symbol](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Absolute_solver) showing in her eye causing the glass to crack, unsettling her about this discovery. Regardless, she is still set on her plan for the extinction of humanity until (<nowiki></nowiki>) comes to her and tells her about the disappearances in the colony after she destroyed T. Despite her dislike of her people, she chooses to return upon the realization that T may have survived the blast and comes upon a mysterious clue with something called the "K̵̛̲̬͍̦̉̉̚͝ͅð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗", further provoking her paranoia.

After barely escaping from [Eldritch T](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eldritch_T), Juliah shows signs of severe trauma and becomes obsessed with the mystery of the K̵̛̲̬͍̦̉̉̚͝ͅð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗ due to her realizing that there is much more going on at hand, with her being scared enough that simply [Y](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Y) touching her shoulder caused her to show genuine terror near the level of their first encounter. It also seems to have lessened her bitter attitude as she immediately ran to hug her father upon seeing him due to Eldritch T having shown a hologram of it having killed him in front of her.

Juliah appears to have reconciled with her father, no longer being aggressive towards him and willing to hear him out when he talks about [Dora](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dora), even if only somewhat. Juliah has also had a very strong bond with and feelings for Y (that may have gradually shifted from platonic to romantic love), being jealous of the other campers that are hanging out with him while she went alone to investigate her mother's past and to look for [Ruqen](/characters/individuals/tritagonists/ruqen/). However, her jealousy, combined with her anxiety over her condition and her fear of abandonment would delve her into insanity, triggering her K̵̛̲̬͍̦̉̉̚͝ͅð̸̩̰̬̪͇̏̈́̈́͊͒k̶̬͙͔͚̪̄͗̆͂̆ṉ̴͓̃̈͛͐̏̏͜ͅͅk̴̛͔̯̖̻͖̋̑̋͊µ̷̢͙̙̩̳͐̔̀̓͋å̵̰̻͙̳͙̇̂͌͑͗ form. After Y confronts her and has a heart-to-heart with her, she has shown to calm down and would reveal hidden depths to her, one that was afraid of being neglected.

While Juliah endures several horrifying encounters throughout the storyline, she eventually overcomes them, becoming willing to endanger herself for the sake of others. At the end of the series, she comes to terms with the colony (admitting she dislikes the living situation less) albeit retaining savage tendencies to terrify other Workers.

## Notable Relationships
Juliah has been described by [D](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/D) as being the "sad purple one" due to "having no friends", as the rest of the [Worker](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Worker)<nowiki/>s are disturbed by her mere presence since she "freaks them out" according to [Lizzo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lizzo), the most popular student in her class in stark contrast to Juliah and how all of her classmates rather put themselves near [Y](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Y) and D, their supposed predators, rather than being near her. This bad behavior towards her is even shown by her only family member, her father Jeramiah, who openly admits that "Doors are [his] real daughter" and shows little hesitation in leaving her to die by the People (Not humans) to save the other colonists.

### Family
#### [Dora](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dora)
Juliah's mother, dora, is believed deceased by the events of the series due to having been euthanized by her husband after being assaulted by the Ugliness of the People, so her relationship with her daughter is very ambiguous due to it being unknown how old Juliah was when Dora had to be "put out of her misery", as claimed by Jeremiah.

According to [Akycha](/characters/individuals/tetragonists/akycha/) (**017**), a former associate of Dora from when she was a test subject in [the Labs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Labs), her first remark upon realizing that Juliah was Nori's daughter was to tell Juliah that she was a lot like her, except Juliah lacking her mother's yellow eyes. As for Juliah, upon learning Akycha was affiliated with her mother, Juliah questioned how they knew each other and what her mother was like.
