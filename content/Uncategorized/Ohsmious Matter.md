**Ohsmius Matter** is a highly exotic form of matter created by the Lizard civilization, characterized by its remarkable ability to disintegrate conventional matter down to its fundamental components, including quarks. Unlike ordinary matter, Ohsmius matter is completely immune to annihilation by antimatter, a trait that makes it uniquely valuable for advanced applications such as weaponry and energy manipulation. This immunity stems from its exotic structure, likely involving an arrangement of subatomic particles or forces that prevent interaction with antimatter. In the presence of antimatter, which would normally annihilate matter by converting it to pure energy, Ohsmius matter remains unaffected.

The process of extracting Ohsmius matter from its naturally occurring form, found in an unknown ore on a Titan-like planet, requires a specialized method that takes advantage of its antimatter immunity. The Titan-like planet, rich in hydrocarbons and with a thick nitrogen-rich atmosphere, presents extreme environmental conditions where Ohsmius-bearing ore can be found. The ore is processed using **antimatter injection**, a method in which antimatter is introduced into a **blast chamber** to annihilate the regular matter portion of the ore, leaving behind the Ohsmius matter.

## Antimatter Annihilation Process
The reaction between antimatter and regular matter follows the equation:
$$
E=mc^2
$$
Where:

- E is the energy released,
- m is the mass of matter and antimatter combined,
- c is the speed of light (3×108 m/s).

When 1 kilogram of antimatter reacts with 1 kilogram of regular matter, the total energy released is:
$$
E = 2 \cdot (1 \, \text{kg}) \cdot (3 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s})^2 = 1.8 \times 10^{17} \, \text{J}
$$
This energy is sufficient to annihilate all regular matter in the ore. However, Ohsmius matter is immune to this annihilation process, which is why it remains intact. Its structure is theorized to have a unique arrangement that prevents it from undergoing typical matter-antimatter reactions. One possible explanation for this immunity could be a different set of forces governing Ohsmius matter's internal stability, making it resistant to the destabilizing effects of antimatter interactions.

## Ohsmius Matter's Role in the Process
After the antimatter blast eradicates the regular ore, the Ohsmius matter remains in a pure, concentrated form. The Lizard civilization's technology leverages the following equations to calculate the amount of antimatter needed to remove all non-Ohsmius material, ensuring the optimal extraction of Ohsmius matter.

Given that the total mass of the ore is more​, and the percentage of Ohsmius matter in the ore is POhsmius​, the mass of regular matter is:
$$
m_{\text{regular}} = m_{\text{ore}} \cdot (1 - P_{\text{Ohsmius}})
$$
The amount of antimatter required to annihilate the regular matter is:
$$
m_{\text{antimatter}} = m_{\text{regular}}
$$
This allows engineers to inject exactly the right amount of antimatter to annihilate all regular matter without wasting resources. If, for example, the ore consists of 2% Ohsmius matter and the total mass of the ore is 100 kg, then:
$$
m_{\text{regular}} = 100 \, \text{kg} \cdot (1 - 0.02) = 98 \, \text{kg}
$$
So, 98 kg of antimatter would be required to process the ore and leave the 2 kg of Ohsmius matter intact.

## Blast Chamber Design
The **blast chamber** itself must be constructed with materials that can withstand the extreme energy release from matter-antimatter annihilation. The chamber's walls are typically reinforced with advanced materials like Ohsmius composites, which are not affected by antimatter. The antimatter is injected through controlled magnetic fields to prevent it from prematurely contacting the chamber walls and annihilating.

Once the antimatter is injected, the regular matter of the ore undergoes annihilation, following:
$$
E_{\text{total}} = (m_{\text{regular}} + m_{\text{antimatter}}) \cdot c^2
$$
The remaining Ohsmius matter is collected after the reaction, as it is impervious to the annihilation process. This purification method is highly efficient, leaving behind Ohsmius matter in its pure form without any of the original ore's impurities.

## Implications of Ohsmius-Antimatter Immunity
The immunity of Ohsmius matter to antimatter is a groundbreaking discovery in the Lizard civilization's technological advancement. This unique characteristic allows for the use of antimatter as a powerful purification tool in otherwise impossible environments, such as the surface of a Titan-like planet, where the ore containing Ohsmius matter is found.

- **Energy Output**: The energy released during the process is carefully managed to ensure it does not damage the equipment or the Ohsmius matter itself.
- **Weaponization Potential**: Due to its antimatter immunity, Ohsmius matter is highly sought after for use in shields and weapons, as it can withstand antimatter-based attacks while causing devastating damage to regular matter.

In summary, Ohsmius matter is an exotic substance immune to antimatter, making it invaluable for high-energy applications. Its extraction from ore on a Titan-like planet involves injecting antimatter into a blast chamber, annihilating the regular matter and leaving behind purified Ohsmius matter. This process showcases the advanced capabilities of the Lizard civilization and their mastery over both antimatter and exotic forms of matter.
