## Overview
Lizard-755 is a lush and verdant world located 15 light-years from Lizard-953-E. It is a mid-sized super-Earth, roughly 1.2 times the size of Earth, with a gravity of 1.04 g, making it only slightly heavier than Earth and highly suitable for humanoid life. The atmosphere is rich in oxygen (34%) and nitrogen (63%), with traces of methane giving the sky a subtle greenish hue. The planet's surface is dominated by massive forests, rolling plains, and expansive freshwater oceans. Its equatorial region experiences year-round temperate climates, while its poles host dense tundra biomes teeming with life.

The geological activity on Lizard-755 is mild, with stable tectonic plates that allow for sprawling alien cities and advanced infrastructure. The planet has few major deserts, as the majority of its landmass is covered in fertile soil, dense vegetation, and towering alien flora resembling bioluminescent trees.

## The Moon: Coleman
Coleman, Lizard-755's only moon, is reminiscent of Saturn's Titan but with striking green-tinted hydrocarbon seas and rivers. Its surface is blanketed by a thick atmosphere of nitrogen and methane, resulting in constant greenish-orange skies. Though the moon is barren of complex life, it harbors abundant natural resources, including hydrocarbon deposits and trace amounts of yabitanite, which the Thrilxots and Lizards jointly mine. Coleman also serves as a base of operations for orbiting fleets, research stations, and outposts for the Lizard-Thrilxot alliance.

## The Thrilxots
The Thrilxots are the dominant native species of Lizard-755 and have become indispensable allies to the Lizards. Physically, they closely resemble humans in their upper bodies but with some distinct differences. Their faces are slightly elongated, and their eyes have a faint emerald glow, likely an evolutionary adaptation to the greenish ambient light of their home planet. Their lower torsos, however, are entirely different — resembling the body of a powerful four-legged mammal, such as a horse or a deer. This centaur-like structure provides them with unmatched speed and agility on the ground.

The Thrilxots have pale skin tones on their upper halves, ranging from silver to light green, and their lower halves are covered in sleek fur, often patterned with stripes or spots unique to each individual. Despite their physical differences, their societal structure and cultural traits are remarkably human-like, with a focus on family, creativity, and technological progress.

## Lifespan & Intelligence
Thrilxots live for about 320 Earth years and are highly intelligent, with a cultural emphasis on science, philosophy, and diplomacy. They are masters of biotechnological advancements, creating organic machines and weapons that integrate seamlessly with nature.

## Government & Alliance with Lizards
The Thrilxots operate under a unified planetary council, with representatives from each region of Lizard-755. After contact with the Lizards centuries ago, they formed an unbreakable alliance, finding common ground in their shared values of environmental preservation and mutual defense against hostile alien forces like the People.

The Thrilxots have become pivotal partners in the Lizardian terraforming projects, offering their expertise in sustainable ecosystems. In return, the Lizards provide advanced technologies and aid in defending Lizard-755 from external threats.

## Combat and Defense
While peaceful by nature, the Thrilxots are formidable warriors when provoked. Their centaur-like physique makes them incredibly fast and difficult to outmaneuver in ground combat. They wield bio-engineered weaponry, such as vine-launching bows and acid-infused organic blades. Many Thrilxots also serve as ground support units in the Lizard military, where their agility and combat prowess shine.

### Cultural Highlights
- **Art & Music:** The Thrilxots are renowned for their organic sculptures and instruments that produce hauntingly beautiful sounds, often inspired by the natural harmony of Lizard-755's ecosystems.
- **Architecture:** Their cities blend seamlessly into the environment, with buildings resembling towering trees or coral-like structures. These cities are often bioluminescent at night, creating a mesmerizing glow visible from space.
- **Religion & Philosophy:** The Thrilxots believe in a cosmic balance, viewing themselves as stewards of their world and allies to the universe's natural order.

## Significance in the Lizardian Galactic Network
Lizard-755 is a critical player in the Lizardian star cluster, serving as both a defensive stronghold and an environmental beacon. The Thrilxots' loyalty and ingenuity have solidified their role as key allies, while their planet's abundant resources make it an invaluable addition to the Lizardian coalition. Together, Lizard-755 and its moon, Coleman, stand as a testament to the harmony between nature and technology, symbolizing hope for the future of the Lizardian alliance.
