The **Elkska Galaxy Confederacy (EGC) also know as LAPAAP (Lizard Allies Protection and Affiliated Planets)** is a highly advanced technocratic alliance of interstellar civilizations centered in the **Elkska Galaxy**. It is led and dominated by the **Lizards**, a powerful species that rose from the hypergravity world of **Lizard-953-E**. The Confederacy is known for its pursuit of cosmic engineering, post-biological transcendence, and strategic colonization of stellar systems.

The Confederacy's **capital and administrative headquarters** is the massive gas giant **Trivarion (Lizard-5757-K)**—an enigmatic world whose eternal storm, known as *Satan's Eye*, serves both as a symbol of power and as a grim warning to dissenters.

## Headquarters: Trivarion (Lizard-5757-K)
- **Classification**: Gas Giant
- **Diameter**: 245,523 km
- **Mass**: 1.76467 × Jupiter
- **Rotation Period**: 16h 34m
- **Atmosphere**: Breathable mix of N₂, O₂, trace Ne and CO₂
- **Gravity**: 1.28 g
- **Magnetosphere**: Exceptionally strong (77 µT)
- **Orbital Position**: 1.2 AU from its parent star in the **Areachon System**

Trivarion is home to **orbital arcologies**, skyhook towers, and storm-anchored floating cities stabilized by graviton counterfields. Its **perpetual cyclone**, *Satan's Eye*, has raged for over 100,000 years, and rumors persist of anomalous entities or extinct civilizations buried deep within the vortex.

Its moons—especially **Ariri**, the only one hosting native life—serve scientific, military, and resource roles. Ariri's toxic, storm-cloaked atmosphere and tectonic activity make it ideal for bioweapons testing and exotic materials research.

## Structure of the Confederacy
- **Ruling Body**: **Technocratic Synod** – a council of elite scientists, engineers, and post-biological intellects
- **Executive Command**: **High Directive**, housed in the **Trivarion Ascendancy Ring** — a vast orbital complex suspended above the gas giant
- **Military Arm**:
  - *Disassembly Drones*
  - *Antimatter Starfleets*
  - *Shielded Tactical Platforms*
- **Law Enforcement**: *AI-supervised planetary ethics modules* with neurojudicial enforcement protocols


## Core Values
- **Technological Ascendancy**
- **Galactic Preservation**
- **Post-Entropy Survival (Microgalaxy Project)**
- **Unity through Efficiency**


## Infrastructure
- **Dyson Sphere Fragments** harness power from multiple stars
- **Tether Arrays & Wormhole Anchors** connect the core worlds
- **Interstellar Data Web** managed by quantum AI nodes
- **Subgalactic Patrols** for monitoring extragalactic threats, like cosmic anomalies and rogue civilizations


## Key Members and Systems
- **Lizard-953-E** – Founding world, still revered as ancestral land
- **Trivarion** – Command Nexus and symbolic heart of the Confederacy
- **Ariri** – Biohazard moon, research outpost, and breeding ground of hexapod fauna
- **Lizard-616 A/B** – Terraforming twins being prepared for long-term habitation
- **Alioska Sector** – Military staging area, contains stellar defense grids


## Scientific Research
- **Pulsar Orbit Alignment** using JJ217
- **Gravitational String Braiding** for stellar propulsion
- **Dark Biosphere Studies** (conducted on Ariri and Trivarion's moons)
- **Synthetic Life Uplift Programs** for controlled species integration


## Culture & Psychology
- **Lizard aesthetic**: harsh geometry, mirror symmetry, glowing plasma forms
- **Languages**:
  - **Primary:** English
  - **Secondary:** Russian, French, Lizard Glyphic
- **Cultural Psyche**: cold logic, survival instinct, reverence for storm and sky
- **Creepy Symbolism**: Trivarion's Eye often carved into hallways, embedded into encryption keys, or stylized into religious-like meditations


## Rumors & Myths
- **The Lizard Ghost of Trivarion**: Some claim a lone Lizard was lost in Satan's Eye… and the eerie radio signals calling "peekaboo… I see you…" are not a malfunction, but a lure.
- **Watchy Window Moon**: A titan-like moon with a translucent surface said to "watch back" when scanned.
- **Red Dot Entity**: Satellite scans suggest something ancient and conscious may lie at the heart of the storm.
