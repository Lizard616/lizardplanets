{{planetaryoverview:Lizard-599-N}}

Lizard-599-N consists of two major landmasses, the continents of Desh'retts and Khemhelt. Less than 40% of the planet is covered in ice, making the entire surface of the world arid tundra, even near the polar regions. The climate of Lizard-599-N is subject to great change, primarily with the presence of mighty earthquakes and torrential snowstorms. Most of these natural occurrences are due to Lizard-599-N's seven moons, which exert great tidal forces against the planet and make subtle alterations to the magnetic field.

Lizard-599-N orbits one sun, Lizard-599-A, a spectral class F4.1 main sequence star. The Lizard-599-Nian day lasts 16 hours and an orbit of the sun takes 4.83 years. Orbiting Lizard-599-N are seven moons; Shashi, Ambika, Hag, Diana, Nguyet, Soma, and Thoth, Hag being ringed.

## Indigenous animals
A species resembling a fox but with 6 legs, known as a Masydon, is indigenous to Lizard-599-N and was domesticated by the Lizards to serve their needs as transportation and possibly food.
