## Overarching qualifications

- Your history on other wikis will be taken into account during applications.
- If you do not have Discord, you are expected to be able to exhibit clear and concise communication with Lizard-Planets and ultimately other moderators.

## Rollbacker — *Open*

Below are the bare minimum qualifications you need to begin to be considered. The only exception to minimum qualifications is if you are approached by a Bureaucrat and offered the position sooner.

- 20 edits
- 2 weeks of editing activity
- Has edited within the past week
- No warnings within the past month
- Very few instances of low-quality edits within the past month

**Below are the optimal qualifications to be considered.**

- 30 edits or more
- 1 month or more of semi-consistent editing activity
- Edits every couple of days or more frequently
- No notices or warnings within the past 3 months
- Consistent, quality edits within the past 3 months
- History of reporting vandalizers via Message Wall or Discord
- Access to Discord
- Rarely active in discussions (or more often)

## Content Moderator — *Open*

Below are the bare minimum qualifications you need to begin to be considered. The only exception to minimum qualifications is if you are approached by a Bureaucrat and offered the position sooner.

- 50 edits
- 1 month of editing activity
- Has edited within the past few days
- No warnings within the past two months
- Very few instances of low-quality edits within the past three months

**Below are the optimal qualifications to be considered.**

- 200 edits or more
- At least 3 months of consistent editing activity
- Edits every day
- No notices or warnings within the past 3 months
- Always consistent, quality edits
- History of reporting vandalizers via Message Wall or Discord
- Access to Discord
- Semi-active in discussions

## Thread Moderator — *Closed*

Below are the bare minimum qualifications you need to begin to be considered. The only exception to minimum qualifications is if you are approached by a Bureaucrat and offered the position sooner.

- 100 posts
- Active for at least 2 weeks
- Has posted within the last two days
- No warnings within the past two months
- Very few deleted posts
- Very few questionable posts
- Decent deleted posts report count

**Below are the optimal qualifications to be considered.**

- 1,000 posts or more
- Active for at least 2 months
- Posts/comments dozens of times a day
- No notices or warnings within the past 6 months
- Always consistent, quality posts that spark responsible discussion
- High deleted posts report count
- History of reporting troublemakers in a courteous way via Message Wall or Discord
- Access to Discord
- No history of minimodding

## Administrator — *Closed*

You may only become an Administrator if you are currently a Moderator. The Administrator role is discussed among staff members in the moderation server and elected via a majority vote with all Bureaucrats, Administrators, Moderators, and Rollbackers present. Administrators must have access to Discord.

One may become a *temporary* Administrator if:

- Staff suspect there may be a raid
- You are trusted and offer to assist in coding or other wiki-building features

## Bureaucrat — *Closed*

Information for this position is currently not applicable as there is no need for another bureaucrat in the foreseeable future. The current bureaucrat(s) are Lizard-Planets and WriteSideofEarth.

## How to apply

Please note that failure to properly apply for these positions, applying without meeting the minimum qualifications, or attempting to apply for a closed position will decrease your chances of receiving any position if you decide to apply again.

### Send Request for Rollbacker or Content Moderator

Optional: Ask Lizard-Planets on his message wall if he thinks you'd be a good fit. However, if you trust your chances, you can skip asking! Go to Discussions and create a post loosely answering all of these questions:

- What is your username? Do you have any nicknames/alternate names you go by?
- Number of edits and posts.
- When did you begin editing?
- Why do you believe you would be a good moderator?
- What is your timezone? How often can you check articles?
- Optional: What previous moderation experience on Fandom, if any? What wikis, how long, and what positions?
- Is there anything else you'd like us to know about your situation, positive or negative?
- Do you have Discord?
- Ping @Lizard-Planets and @WriteSideofEarth
