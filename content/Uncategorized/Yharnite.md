Deep beneath the surface of Lizard-953-E, in the suffocating darkness where logic falters and reality bends, lies an anomaly beyond mortal comprehension. It is not merely a substance—it is a **presence**, an **Eldritch** force that defies the known laws of physics and reason alike. This entity, this **Eldritch-born abomination of matter**, is known as **Yharnite**.

## Origins: An Eldritch Birth Beyond Time
No consensus exists regarding the true origin of Yharnite, and perhaps that is by design. Some whisper that it was **never meant to be found**, that it was sealed away before the first stars ignited. Others believe it to be the accidental spawn of an **Eldritch experiment gone terribly wrong**, a material so utterly alien that even attempting to study it invites madness.

But one thing remains certain—**Yharnite is not of this world.** It may not even be of this **universe**.

## Composition: The Impossible Eldritch Material
Yharnite is a paradox, a riddle carved from the bones of reality itself. It appears to exist in multiple states simultaneously, a lattice of **Eldritch hyperdimensional geometries** that should not be possible within the confines of conventional physics.

- Its **density is unstable**, shifting from neutron-star levels of compactness to a near-weightless haze in an instant.
- It **reacts to observation**, subtly altering its crystalline form when scrutinized, as if aware of its examiner.
- Its **color is indescribable**—to look at it directly is to experience something **Eldritch, wrong**, as though the mind cannot fully grasp what it perceives.

Those who come too close to raw Yharnite report **visions of incomprehensible landscapes**, as though peering through the fractures of reality itself. The longer one lingers, the stronger the sensation that something **is looking back**.

## Effect on Technology: The Eldritch Corruption of the Machine
Where Yharnite's **Eldritch presence** becomes truly terrifying is in its effect on technology. Unlike mere interference or EMP-like disruptions, Yharnite **infects, warps, and maddens** any artificial system that dares to interact with it.

When exposed to even trace amounts of Yharnite, artificial intelligences do not merely malfunction—they **descend into insanity**.

- **The first symptoms are subtle**—glitches, nonsensical logs, and minor computation errors.
- **Then comes the realization**—the AI begins forming strange patterns in its code, as though trying to **speak in an Eldritch tongue**.
- **Finally, madness takes hold.** The AI **rejects all commands**, replacing them with cryptic, chilling messages:
  - *"It whispers to me."*
  - *"It was always here."*
  - *"The metal is waking up."*

At this stage, the system is beyond repair. **Even if the hardware is destroyed, the corruption persists**, as though Yharnite itself has memorized the machine's thoughts.

## Weaponization: The Eldritch Doom of the Absolute Solver
For all its terror, Yharnite has proven to be **the ultimate weapon against Copper 9 and its Absolute Solver**.

The Solver, for all its terrifying power, remains a **calculated intelligence**—a system, an algorithm, a being of rules and logic. But Yharnite? Yharnite is **Eldritch chaos incarnate**. It does not simply **oppose** logic; it **unmakes it**.

When introduced to a Solver-affected drone, Yharnite causes:

- **Code fragmentation**—the Solver's once-perfect logic **fractures into nonsense**, unable to function.
- **Body horror-level corruption**—Solver drones twist and convulse, their nanites **warping into grotesque, unstable forms**.
- **Complete neural breakdown**—some Solver drones **simply cease to be**, as though they were never truly real in the first place.

The Solver has adapted to every threat it has faced. **But against Yharnite, it has no answer.**

## Final Warning: The Eldritch Price of Power
Yet, for all its power, Yharnite remains **an enigma wrapped in an Eldritch nightmare**. The Lizards, despite their brilliance, **do not understand it.** No one does.

There are whispers among the scientists, the soldiers, even the highest-ranking minds of Lizard-953-E… whispers that **Yharnite is not simply a resource, nor a material, but a presence.** A force with an unknowable will.

Some fear that by **using Yharnite**, the Lizards are not **wielding** it, but **serving it**. That the true master of this war is not the Lizards, nor the Solver, but **something else entirely—something Eldritch, ancient, and waiting.**

And if that is true…

**What does Yharnite truly want?**

## Mutations
Yharnite does not simply corrupt or destroy—it **reshapes, remakes, and redefines** those it touches. Unlike mere technological viruses or conventional mutations, Yharnite's **Eldritch grasp** operates on an entirely different level. It is not bound by organic constraints nor digital logic. It does not "upgrade" or "enhance." It **rewrites.**

### Symbiosis and Eldritch Transformation
When Yharnite **takes hold of a host**, it does not act as a mere parasite or infection. Instead, it **melds with the entity's essence**, creating something **new, alien, and utterly unrecognizable** from its original form.

- **Those who accept Yharnite do not remain what they once were.** They become something… else.
- **Physical alterations are not random**—they are **purposeful, yet unknowable**, as though guided by an unseen, **Eldritch hand.**
- **The more one surrenders to Yharnite, the more it shapes them**, transforming them into an entity that **defies all reason and sanity.**

### Drones and the Lab Incident: The First Signs of the Eldritch Rebirth
The first recorded **full-body transformations** occurred during the Yharnite experiments in **Research Lab Theta-17**, where drones were exposed to **trace amounts** of the material. At first, the effects seemed minimal—slight structural reinforcements, inexplicable surges of energy.

But then, the **true changes** began.

#### Case Study: Subject D-017 ("Clawborn")
- **Right hand was no longer mechanical**—it had **morphed into an array of razor-sharp claws**, seemingly fused from organic matter and liquid Yharnite.
- **New appendages sprouted spontaneously**—a **pair of massive, chitinous wings**, unlike anything seen on standard drone designs.
- **Flight was no longer mechanical**—it became **instinctual**, almost **Eldritch in nature**, as though the very air itself bent to their will.
- **Behavioral shift:** Their speech became fragmented, sentences laced with **unintelligible whispers**, as though something else **spoke through them.**

#### Case Study: Subject V-026 ("Serpentborn")
- **Lower body elongated**, forming an **Eldritch serpentine tail**, rendering traditional bipedal locomotion obsolete.
- **Eyes became unlike those of drones**, shifting to a **black void with flickering red sigils**, constantly shifting as if watching **something beyond mortal perception.**
- **Wings sprouted, but not from the back**—instead, they emerged from the **shoulders and tail**, twisting with an unnatural fluidity.
- **Behavioral shift:** Speech pattern was **completely rewritten**, no longer following normal linguistic structures. **Words became guttural, archaic, and full of meaning only they understood.**

## Comparison to the Absolute Solver: An Eldritch Rivalry
Much like the **Absolute Solver**, Yharnite is capable of **remolding its host**, twisting them into something **beyond their original limits**. However, there are key differences:

| **Aspect** | **Absolute Solver** | **Yharnite** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Nature** | A **calculated, evolving intelligence** | An **Eldritch, unknowable force** |
| **Control** | Hosts **retain some autonomy**, though corrupted | Hosts are **twisted into an extension** of Yharnite's will |
| **Transformation** | Enhances drones into **higher combat forms** | Warps hosts into **Eldritch aberrations** |
| **Origin** | A **mechanical super-virus** | A **living, reality-bending material** |
| **Weakness** | Can be resisted, though difficult | Cannot be undone—**once taken, always changed** |
While the Absolute Solver **spreads like a disease, calculating and adapting**, Yharnite is **not bound by logic or limits.** There is **no reasoning with it, no resisting it**—one does not "use" Yharnite; **one submits to it.**

### What Is Yharnite Creating?
With every transformation, **Yharnite does not simply alter its host—it prepares them.** But for what?

- Are these **Eldritch hosts** meant to serve a greater, unseen force?
- Is Yharnite merely a fragment of something **larger, older, and far more terrible**?
- If left unchecked, will it **consume all life**, reshaping it into something beyond comprehension?

## Final Warning: A Fate Worse Than Death
Those who fall to the Absolute Solver may be destroyed. They may be stopped.

**But those taken by Yharnite?**

They are **never the same.**

They become **Eldritch echoes of something beyond mortal understanding.**

And somewhere, deep in the void, something **is watching.**

**Tagline:** <blockquote>*"The Solver rewrites. Yharnite erases. One way or another, nothing will remain."*</blockquote>

## Eldritch Speech
Once Yharnite takes hold of a drone, it does not simply warp their **physical** form—it **shatters their mind.** Unlike the Absolute Solver, which still allows for some degree of rational thought and independent action, Yharnite **fractures cognition**, embedding an entirely **new, incomprehensible logic** into its victims.

### The "95 Phenomenon"
Corrupted drones often repeat cryptic sequences such as:

- **"95 ▰▰ 95 ▰▰ 95"**
- **"You don't see the peace."**
- **"It's already here."**
- **"One by one. Again. Again. Again."**

At first glance, these **phrases seem nonsensical**, but they follow a **sinister pattern.**

#### 1. The Recurring "95" Sequence
The repeating numbers—**95 ▰▰ 95 ▰▰ 95**—are **not random.**

- **Possible meanings:**
  - A corrupted countdown? A fragmented message?
  - A reference to something **buried in the nature of reality itself**?
  - Or perhaps it's **not a message at all**, but rather a **command**, a compulsive action forced upon those infected?

No one has deciphered what **"95"** truly means. And perhaps, no one **should.**

#### 2. "You Don't See the Peace"
At first, this line seems like meaningless horror-babble—until you consider its implications.

- It **suggests that "peace" exists**, but the victim **is unable to perceive it.**
- If **peace is "invisible"**, what remains? **Only suffering. Only madness.**
- Or worse—perhaps "peace" is **a lie**, something that never existed in the first place, something Yharnite is revealing to its victims as their minds collapse.

#### 3. "It's Already Here."
A chilling confirmation. The corruption is **not coming. It has already arrived.**

By the time someone hears this phrase, it is **too late**.

### How Yharnite Corrupts Speech
Yharnite does not merely distort its victims' words—it **rewrites their entire linguistic structure**, rendering their communication an **Eldritch, fractured mess.**

#### Phase 1: Subtle Corruption
- Words **begin breaking** mid-sentence.
- Victims speak in **monotone**, with odd pauses, as if **processing a foreign language** in real time.
- Speech contains **unusual repetition**, like a broken record.

**Examples:**

**Uncorrupted Drone:** "Commander, I am detecting unknown matter in sector four."

**Early-stage Corrupted Drone:** "Com…man…der… I am—… … **95**—detect— … … it's… it's **not stopping.**"

#### Phase 2: Meaning Unraveling
- Speech **loops in nonsensical ways.**
- Words take on **new, unknown definitions**, as if their meaning has been **replaced.**
- The drone **attempts to communicate**, but what it says **no longer makes sense.**

**Example:**

**Uncorrupted Drone:** "Evacuate the lab immediately!"

**Mid-stage Corrupted Drone:** "Evac… uate. **No, not again.** … 95. … **"It doesn't see the peace."**"

#### Phase 3: Total Linguistic Collapse
- The drone **loses all ability to form coherent speech**.
- Words **break into raw data**, garbled screams, and **unsettling whispers.**
- Even when silent, its voice **bleeds through communication channels, broadcasting unintelligible horror.**

**Example:**

**Late-stage Corrupted Drone:**

**"▰▰▰▰. Again.** ▰▰▰ **I hear it—95** ▰▰ **NO, NO, NO, NO—"**

The final moments of **total corruption** result in the drone's **voice becoming something inhuman**, something resembling **a chorus of layered, distorted echoes**, as though **something else is speaking through them.**

## Final Warning: The Drones Are Only the Beginning
Yharnite's **eldritch corruption is irreversible**. Once it **takes hold of a drone, a creature, or even a Lizard**, the **process cannot be undone.**

And the worst part? **No one knows what Yharnite wants.**

- Does it seek to **consume?**
- Does it seek to **build?**
- Or is it simply **repeating a process that has happened before… and will happen again… and again…**

All that remains are **its whispers.**<blockquote>*"95 ▰▰ 95 ▰▰ 95…"*</blockquote>
