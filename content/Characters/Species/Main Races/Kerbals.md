**Kerbals** are small, green humanoid beings from the *Kerbal Space Program* universe. They are known for their curiosity, enthusiasm for space exploration, and often reckless approach to science and engineering. Here are some key characteristics of Kerbals:

## Physical Traits
- **Height:** Around 0.75 to 1 meter tall (shorter than humans).
- **Skin:** Green, smooth, and somewhat rubbery-looking.
- **Head:** Large, round heads with big, expressive eyes and a wide mouth.
- **Body:** Short and stocky with stubby limbs, giving them a cartoonish appearance.
- **Hair:** Some Kerbals, like Jebediah Kerman, have visible hair, but most are bald.
- **Durability:** Surprisingly tough—Kerbals can survive high g-forces, crash landings, and vacuum exposure for short periods.

## Biological & Environmental Adaptations
- **Breathable Atmosphere Compatibility:** Kerbals can breathe **Lizard-953-E's** atmosphere without issue, meaning they are tolerant of **high helium and oxygen levels**.
- **Gravity Adaptation:** Kerbals have evolved (or were designed) to handle different gravities well, making them fairly adaptable to Lizard-953-E's **1.89 g** gravity.
- **Metabolism & Stamina:** Despite their small size, Kerbals can work tirelessly for long periods, though they have a tendency to panic when things go wrong.

## Behavior & Intelligence
- **Curious & Experimental:** Kerbals love space travel and experimentation, sometimes ignoring safety protocols.
- **Fearless (Mostly):** Some, like *Jebediah Kerman*, show no fear, while others panic easily under stress.
- **Ingenious, But Chaotic:** They are capable of engineering impressive spacecraft, yet their designs often explode due to questionable engineering choices.

Since they can breathe **Lizard-953-E's** atmosphere, Kerbals would likely be fascinated by the planet and eager to explore it—though their usual trial-and-error approach might get them into trouble with the **Lizards**!
