**Thoth** also known as **Lizard-599-N** **IV** (provisional designation: **S/3071 9537 EL<sub>77</sub>9**) is the smaller of the seven moons of Lizard-599-N. It is roughly 3747 km across. Thoth orbits Lizard-599-N every 34.8 days at a distance of 349,300 km.
