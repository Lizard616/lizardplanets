On the Lizard-Planets Wiki, user pages are in general used as a user "profile", with information on a user's activities on the wiki, ranging from what pages they edit, what they are editing, what rights they hold, what groups they are a member etc. However, Lizard-Planets Wiki does have a number of policies that apply to user pages.

- **No Creative-Commons *Lizard-Planets* images unless already used elsewhere** - Creative-Commons images of *Lizard-Planets*, that is, any screenshots and images whose copyrights belong to Lizard-Planets, can only be used on user pages if the image is already in use elsewhere on the wiki. In short, this means that users should not upload *Lizard-Planets* screenshots specifically for their user pages.
- **No excessive editing of the user page in comparison to other pages and articles** - The user page is intended to be a profile of a wiki editor. Lizard-Planets Wiki is not a social networking site and it is expected that users who wish to edit regularly not solely focus on their user page.
- **No blacklists of other users** - Users should not make a list of users they dislike or wish to discredit on their user page. Lizard-Planets Wiki should keep a positive editing environment and users should not blast other users on their user page.
