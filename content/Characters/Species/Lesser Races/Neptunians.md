## Overview
The Neptunians are the native inhabitants of the [Neptunian Empire](/uncategorized/neptunian-empire/) and it's home planet, Neptune.  They are an hybrid species, formed as an result of the [Lizardian](/characters/individuals/protagonists/lizardian-empire/) exodus to Neptune, which happened in 2105 after the Neptunians and the [Lizardians](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) came into contact with each other. They are technologically advanced, being closely similar to the Lizardians, but on an interplanetary scale. They are friendly and helpful, but when it comes to war, they don't back down, instead fighting until the very end.



## History and features of the Neptunians
The Neptunians are human-like beings, which is thanks to their human origins. 

They share many of their external and internal features with the [Humans](/characters/species/main-races/humans/) and the Lizardians and as such, it can be easy to confuse them with said species.

However, they have their own unique features, including but not limited to:

- Peach-colored skin (This is due to their homeworld, Neptune, being far away from the Sun, hence they don't have a lot of melanin),
- Lightly colored hair (Same thing as with their skin),
- Bio- and nanotech integration (Mostly thanks to the Lizardian-Human collaboration) and
- Near-complete resistance to the Absolute Solver and other biomaterial-controlling digital threats.

Their history ties deep with that of the Neptunian Empire. Before the Lizardian Exodus of 2105, the Neptunians were similar to their fellow humans, except for the fact that they survive extremly cold weather due to them being used to the cold weather of Neptune. However with Lizardians arriving on Neptune in 2105, things changed. While the intergration of the Lizardians was slow at first due to cultural differences, it went smoothly later on. Because of this, the Neptunians are partially Lizardians themselves and as such, they hate all beings who are hostile towards them (Such as the [People Aliens](/characters/species/main-races/people-not-humans/) and the [Oniixides](/characters/species/lesser-races/oniixide/)). Since then, the Neptunians fought the Solver back and defeated it twice. Their main enemies are the Voiders, an hybrid species created out of people who were rebels of the Solar Uprising (Jovians, Saturnians and Uranians, as well as Plutonians), as well as People aliens, Solver and [Shadow MATRIX Drones](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shadow_MATRIX_Drones). The two species hate each other for years, starting from 2130 when the People aliens attacked Neptune and the Neptunians took planets from them. Since then, relations between the two species remain tense, with smaller conficts breaking out on important areas.
