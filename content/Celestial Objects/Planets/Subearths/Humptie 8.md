Humptie 8 is a harsh, inhospitable world with a diameter of 8,008 km and a mass just 0.2888 times that of Earth. The planet's low gravity of 0.183 m/s² gives it a peculiar, almost weightless feel, where even the immense Humpties move with an eerie slowness, their massive forms floating gently through the air. The atmosphere is thick with hydrogen sulfide (H2S) and methane (CH4), creating a toxic, suffocating environment that is difficult for most life forms to survive in.

The surface of Humptie 8 is rugged and barren, with vast stretches of desolate plains, jagged mountains, and deep, dark craters. The planet's weak gravity means that any movement, whether by the Humpties or other objects, is slow and deliberate. The skies are a murky, yellowish hue due to the presence of methane and sulfur compounds in the atmosphere, casting an eerie glow over the barren landscape.

Inhabited by the Humpties, the planet features towering, fortress-like cities designed to withstand the harsh conditions. These cities are massive and sprawling, constructed with sturdy, thick materials to cope with the toxic air and low gravity. The Humpties' gluttonous nature has led to heavy pollution and resource depletion, and their cities reflect their overconsumption with sprawling, inefficient infrastructure. Despite the planet's challenges, the Humpties thrive here, their prideful and destructive nature driving them to expand and conquer other worlds.
