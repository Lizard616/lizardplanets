{{galacticinfo:Elkska}}

The **Elkska Galaxy** is an artificially constructed microgalaxy and the central hub of the Lizardian Empire. With a diameter of **5,320 light-years** and housing approximately **13,000 stars**, Elkska is a compact yet sophisticated galaxy designed to ensure the survival and prosperity of the Lizards in the face of cosmic challenges, including the Heat Death of the Universe.

## Astrographical Specifications
- **Galaxy Type:** Engineered Dwarf Galaxy
- **Diameter:** **5,320 light-years**
- **Stellar Population:** **~13,000 stars**, predominantly high-metallicity Population II stars for stability and resource extraction.
- **Current Location:** The **Elkska Galaxy** serves as the **primary location of all the Lizardian Empire's star systems**, carefully relocated here for security and sustainability.

## Structural and Dynamic Features
- **Compact Design:**
  - Despite its small size compared to typical galaxies, Elkska is meticulously engineered for maximum efficiency. The dense distribution of stars allows for short interstellar travel distances, reducing the energy expenditure for transportation and communication.
- **Central Organization:**
  - Stars are arranged in an optimized, semi-elliptical pattern surrounding **Pepper**, the supermassive black hole anchoring the galaxy.
  - Strategic gravitational stabilization prevents stellar collisions and maintains long-term galactic balance.

## Energy Systems and Technology
- **Dyson Swarm Utilization:**
  - Key stars in the galaxy are surrounded by Dyson swarms, allowing for near-complete energy harvesting to fuel the Lizardian Empire's advanced technologies.
- **Artificial Star Formation:**
  - Elkska is equipped with engineered star-forming regions to replenish its stellar population over billions of years.
- **Mobile Galaxy Infrastructure:**
  - The galaxy's position is not fixed; it is equipped with **stellar engines** that allow controlled movement within the universe. This mobility ensures that Elkska remains in resource-rich or strategically advantageous locations while avoiding cosmic dangers.

## Key Galactic Attributes
- **Pepper's Role:**
  -  At the galaxy's core, **Pepper**, a supermassive black hole, acts as the gravitational anchor and a vital energy source for antimatter production and dark energy manipulation.
- **Width-to-Stellar Ratio:**
  - With its **5,320 light-year width**, Elkska's stellar density is far higher than a typical galaxy, ensuring a tight-knit empire with rapid interstellar communication and defense capabilities.

## Societal and Strategic Importance
The **Elkska Galaxy** is the heart of the Lizardian Empire's power, innovation, and survival strategy. By consolidating their star systems into this compact microgalaxy, the Lizards have ensured unmatched territorial control, efficient resource distribution, and centralized governance.

It is also a symbol of resilience and foresight, representing their determination to overcome cosmic entropy and secure a future for their species, even as the universe approaches its inevitable decline.
