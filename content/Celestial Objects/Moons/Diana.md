**Diana** is the second largest moon orbiting the exoplanet Lizard-599-N in the Lizard-599 System. It's sister moon, Hag, is the largest of the Lizard-599-N satellite system.

## Trivia
- It is named after Diana from Greek mythology, Diana was the goddess of hunting.
