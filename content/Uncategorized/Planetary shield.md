**Planetary shields**, also known as a **planetary shield generator**, were large Energy shields designed to protect entire [planet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Planet)s. These shields were used by the Lizardian Empire to protect important Planets such as [Lizard-953-E](/celestial-objects/planets/super-earths/lizard-953-e/) or [P3X-996](/celestial-objects/planets/super-earths/p3x-996/), and by the First Order to protect their base of operations/[superweapon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Superweapon), Nicoll Dyson Beam or entire stars.

## Description
Planetary shields were monolithic Energy shields that enveloped entire [planet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Planet)s. Designed to protect important installations from attack, planetary shields were impregnable against Open fire and any other cosmic Attack.

The planetary shield that surrounded Lizard-953-E had a fractional refresh rate, preventing any spacecraft traveling slower than lightspeed from transitioning into the planet's atmosphere.
