Nestled within the heart of the impenetrable jungle on Planet Lizard-967-X7-Kokushibo lies the clandestine research facility. Veiled by dense foliage and shielded from prying eyes, this facility serves as the clandestine epicenter of the Lizards' most audacious experiments.

{{gallery:Clandestine Research Facility}}

Concealed beneath a canopy of towering trees, clandestine research facility boasts a labyrinth of interconnected laboratories, each chamber shrouded in an aura of mystery and scientific intrigue. Within these dimly lit confines, advanced machinery hums with purpose, while the air is thick with the scent of ozone and the faint crackle of energy fields.

Central to the Facility's operations is the Serpentarium Wing, a specialized section devoted to the augmentation of human physiology with mammal traits. Here, test subjects are ushered into sterile chambers adorned with arcane symbols and pulsating energy conduits, where they undergo a metamorphosis beyond human comprehension.

Under the watchful gaze of the Lizards' scientists, the subjects are subjected to a series of transformative procedures, their bodies infused with a cocktail of exotic compounds and genetic manipulations. Within the confines of these chambers, the very fabric of reality seems to warp and bend, as the boundaries between human and reptilian blur.

In one corner of the Serpentarium Wing lies the Hyperbaric Helium Chamber, a marvel of Lizards' engineering. Within its transparent confines, test subjects are immersed in an atmosphere rich in helium, their lungs gradually acclimated to the alien gas that permeates the atmosphere of Planet Lizard-967-X7-Kokushibo.

Yet, beyond the sterile confines of the laboratories, a sense of unease pervades the corridors of the clandestine research facility

. Whispers of ethereal experiments and hidden agendas echo through its halls, leaving even the most seasoned scientists to ponder the true nature of their research on this enigmatic world.
