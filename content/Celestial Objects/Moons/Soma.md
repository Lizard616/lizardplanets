**Soma**, also known as **Lizard-599-N VI**, is a prograde moon and is the fourth largest natural satellite of the outer planet, Lizard-599-N. Is is also the sixth largest moon in the Lizard-599 System, just behind Hag. This however is the only moon known in the Lizard-599 System to have a dense atmosphere and is even believed to have surface water, the only other body to have received this classification after Lizard-599-N.
