Yurcat tree is an impressive, large tree that can grow up to 75 feet tall and 8 feet in diameter. Learn more about the unique features of this magnificent tree. The Yurcat tree is a very common, modest plant and can be found in most subarctic regions. It blooms twice a year, for 1 month. It has large, diamond shaped leaves, which are usually lime green. It also grows large flowers, which can be dark red and dark grey. These plants grow in small numbers, but it's fairly easy to control and maintain their growth. They have no use, but smell nice. As a defense mechanism the Yurcat tree tastes horrible, when eaten raw. They rely on winds to carry their seeds away to reproduce. Once pollinated, they grow small, inedible nuts.
