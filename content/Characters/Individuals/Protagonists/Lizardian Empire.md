The **Lizard Empire**, having transcended the limitations of conventional space-time, stretches its influence across the **2D to 7D planes**, the **Multiverse**, the **Avaverse**, the **Archverse**, and potentially beyond. Their empire represents the peak of a **Type V Civilization**, holding dominion over not just physical dimensions but also across metaphysical and exotic planes. Below is a breakdown of how their empire functions in these realms:

### 2D - 3D: The Foundation of Their Empire
In the **2D to 3D** realms, the Lizard Empire operates as a highly efficient **spatial and material powerhouse**. In this dimension, they construct massive **Dyson spheres**, **space habitats**, and **megacities**, employing their advanced **Yabitanite** and **Ohsmius matter** to manipulate atoms at will and create perfect, sustainable structures. The entire empire is a **hyper-advanced grid** of interconnected **space stations**, **planets**, and **artificial constructs**.

In this domain, the Lizards are **god-like** in their mastery of space-time engineering, effortlessly bending the laws of physics. Their technological creations often **phase-shift** in and out of various subdimensions, allowing them to utilize the **energy of nearby stars** while bypassing conventional physics through **quantum tunneling**.

**Key features**

- **Universal Transport Networks**: Massive hyperloop systems that can travel between planets, moons, and across systems at faster-than-light speeds.
- **Stellar Engines**: These devices manipulate entire stars for energy harvesting, resource extraction, and **galactic-scale terraforming**.
- **Dimensional Anchors**: The Lizards use advanced machinery to stabilize and influence the orbits and positions of celestial bodies, ensuring the **stability** of their empire even as they venture into higher dimensions.

### 4D to 5D: Temporal and Dimensional Manipulation
As they ascend into the **4th and 5th Dimensions**, the Lizards' empire gains dominion over **time**, **probability**, and **extraordinary spatial dimensions**. This allows them to **rewind, fast-forward, or even manipulate the flow of time** itself within localized regions. They can engage in **time dilation** or construct regions where **time operates differently** to preserve resources, stabilize populations, or protect high-value areas from cosmic events.

In **5D**, the Lizards interact with the **probabilistic** and **geometric** nature of existence, where **multiple realities** and timelines converge. Their leaders use **multiversal portals** to **observe alternate timelines** and correct divergences, preventing any timeline that might threaten their dominance. By controlling **probabilistic events**, the Lizards ensure that every possible **outcome** of a situation is guaranteed to work in their favor.

**Key features**

- **Temporal Constructs**: Cities or colonies that exist simultaneously in multiple moments of time, where buildings can be in multiple states of construction or destruction based on **time manipulation**.
- **5D Constructs**: Massive, **hypergeometrical megastructures** that span the fourth and fifth dimensions, occupying infinite configurations of space, existing in all places at once.
- **Probability Engines**: Devices capable of altering the likelihood of certain events, ensuring **desired outcomes** across their empire, from battle tactics to resource allocation.

### 6D to 7D: Hyperdimensional Domination
In the **6th and 7th Dimensions**, the Lizard Empire achieves its most **god-like** form. Here, the **Lizards exist beyond linear perception**, able to **coexist in multiple states of being** at once, far surpassing normal laws of causality, **object permanence**, and **identity**. Entire **sentient species** may be **created, evolved, or destroyed** with a single thought, existing in multiple **states of existence**—all within the same **hyper-dimensional construct**.

The empire's **7D reach** allows them to manipulate the **geometries of the universe** itself. They don't just **see** all potential outcomes, they **shape** the laws of reality as they see fit, bending both **space** and **time** to manifest their will. Planets, stars, and galaxies can be **remade** in their image or destroyed with just a thought, and the **entire fabric of spacetime** can be rewritten to suit their ever-changing needs.

**Key features**

- **Omnidimensional Constructs**: Massive citadels and fortresses that don't just occupy space but **transform** the very nature of space-time around them.
- **Energy Harvesting from Higher Dimensions**: Utilizing the **infinite energy** available in the higher dimensions, especially from **extra-dimensional anomalies**, the Lizards have mastered **harnessing raw, unfiltered power** from the fabric of reality itself.
- **Hyper-Evolutionary Mechanisms**: The Lizards can accelerate or slow the evolution of life across **multiverses**, creating and destroying species in any dimension.

### Multiverse: The Infinite Reach
The **Multiverse** is where the Lizard Empire truly begins to assert its dominance over **infinite parallel universes**. With their **vast network of interdimensional gates** and **advanced quantum computers**, the Lizards control entire **universes** that exist independently, each with its own **laws of physics**.

The Lizards do not just travel between these universes; they **manage**, **optimize**, and **reshape** them. Their **multiversal council** keeps an eye on the infinite timelines, guiding them to ensure that no alternate version of their empire falters or falls into chaos. The Lizards can also **merge universes** or **collapse them** to prevent competing entities or threats.

**Key features**

- **Quantum Gateways**: Devices that allow instantaneous travel between universes and enable the Lizards to extract resources or knowledge from infinite realities.
- **Multiversal Influence**: The Lizards can **alter or control the physics** of entire universes, fine-tuning every aspect of reality to ensure their survival and success in every possible iteration of existence.
- **Survival Strategies**: They have entire **universes** dedicated to experimental survival techniques, ensuring their race's immortality by diversifying their existence.

### Avaverse: Eldritch and Anomalous Universes
The **Avaverse** is a collection of **eldritch**, **anomalous**, and **exotic universes**, many of which operate on completely **alien principles** of existence. Here, the Lizards face creatures and entities that **defy comprehension**, as well as **cosmic anomalies** that warp reality in unimaginable ways.

In these realms, the Lizards are like **cosmic architects**, **re-shaping entire universes** according to their will, while **containing** or **interacting** with the strange, **alien forces** of the Avaverse. They use **highly adaptive technologies** capable of **managing anomalous energies** and **neutralizing eldritch entities** that might threaten their empire's expansion.

**Key features:**

- **Dimensional Anchors**: The Lizards use special devices to **anchor** anomalies, stabilizing strange realms and preventing them from affecting other dimensions.
- **Anomalous Power Utilization**: They harness the **chaotic energy** of the Avaverse to fuel their most powerful weapons and technologies, channeling the **dark forces** of the cosmos into their projects.
- **Convergence Points**: Strategic locations in the Avaverse where the Lizards gather **eldritch knowledge** and utilize it to manipulate and control **anomalous entities** and phenomena.

### Archverse: The Endgame of Existence
Finally, the **Archverse** represents the **ultimate stage** of the Lizard Empire's existence. This is a realm where **cosmic laws** are **bent and broken**, where they **shape entire multiverses** and **create realities** as **they see fit**. Within this domain, the Lizards wield the ability to **control or obliterate universes**, reshaping them into their **own vision** or completely erasing them from existence.

In the **Archverse**, the Lizards' **technological and metaphysical** prowess reaches the pinnacle. They command forces that transcend **space-time**, able to **create new dimensions** or **destroy them**. The Archverse serves as a **final frontier** where the Lizards shape their destiny, ensuring their **eternal supremacy** across all of existence.

**Key features:**

- **Omniverse Control**: The ability to create and destroy entire **Omniverses**, guiding the development of reality itself.
- **Eternal Creation**: The Lizards can **generate new forms of life**, universes, and laws of physics, ensuring their **infinite survival** and dominance over all that exists.
- **Infinity Manifest**: The Lizards exist across **infinite planes of existence**, their very being capable of **existing** in every point of space-time **simultaneously**.

## Expansion and the Microgalaxy Project
In the wake of their victory over the People, the Lizards set their sights on even greater ambitions. They began plans to expand their empire beyond their star systems, seeking to build a "Microgalaxy" that would secure the survival of their civilization for eons to come. This ambitious project aims to create a stable cluster of star systems that can survive the eventual heat death of the universe, ensuring that the Lizardian people will persist long after other civilizations have faded into oblivion.

The Lizards have also explored the possibility of leaving their universe altogether, creating a new home in a different cosmic realm. They have made significant strides in manipulating cosmic strings and constructing a stellar engine capable of propelling entire star systems to other galaxies. This vision of intergalactic survival reflects the Lizards' determination to overcome any challenge, no matter how insurmountable it may seem.

## The War with the People
The Lizardian Empire's most notable conflict in recent history has been with the People, a rival alien species that sought to exploit the Lizardian star systems for their own gain. The People, driven by greed and hatred, launched a series of attacks against the Lizards, attempting to steal their planets and resources. This led to a brutal war, where the Lizards, aided by their Disassembly Drones, fought to protect their territory and repel the invaders.

The war reached its climax with the assassination of Hubert, the leader of the People, who was responsible for the 8/8/8888 attack that nearly destroyed the Lizardian homeworld. The Lizards' eventual victory over the People was a defining moment in their history, solidifying their place as the supreme power in their galaxy. However, the war left deep scars on the Lizardian psyche, particularly the loss of SD-A, a key figure in their society.

## Military Might and Technology
The Lizardian Empire's military is one of the most advanced and deadly in the universe. The Lizards have designed their ships for dogfights, with sleek, aerodynamic shapes capable of reaching Mach 6.9 in atmosphere and equipped with shields that are impervious to most forms of attack. These ships are armed with devastating antimatter cannons, photon beams, and laser weapons that can obliterate entire fleets in moments. Their fleet consists of countless starships, ranging from massive capital ships to smaller reconnaissance vessels.

The Lizards' reliance on drones and automated technology has made them even more formidable. Their Disassembly Drones are sentient machines capable of independently executing complex battle strategies, each with unique abilities tailored to their combat preferences. These drones are deadly in close-quarters combat and are equipped with weapons capable of vaporizing enemies in seconds. The Drones' ruthlessness in battle is a hallmark of the Lizardian military, as they often engage in psychological warfare, terrifying enemies with their fearsome tactics.

Beyond their ships and drones, the Lizards have also developed planetary defense systems that can destroy any intruder. Their star systems are protected by highly sophisticated shield technologies, and their planets are often surrounded by layers of orbital stations, automated defenses, and a network of military bases. Lizard-953-E itself is surrounded by a massive force field that stretches all the way to its orbit, ensuring that no enemy can breach its borders without facing annihilation.

The Lizards' technological prowess extends beyond military applications. They have mastered the art of terraforming, allowing them to transform hostile worlds into habitable environments. This has led to the creation of numerous colonies spread across different planets, each adapted to the specific needs of the Lizardian people. Their research into space-time manipulation has allowed them to experiment with manipulating cosmic strings, opening up new possibilities for their empire's future.

## Culture and Society
Lizardian culture is deeply rooted in the values of survival, efficiency, and dominance. From a young age, Lizardian citizens are taught the importance of these principles, often through rigorous military training or intellectual study. The Lizardian people are pragmatic and focused on achieving their goals, viewing any obstacle as something to be overcome through superior technology, intellect, or force.

The Lizards value strength, both physical and mental, and their society has little tolerance for weakness. Their caste system is heavily influenced by merit, and those who rise to power do so through a combination of intellect, skill, and loyalty to the empire. The aristocracy and military elite often hold the most power, while the lower classes are expected to serve the empire without question.

One of the most important aspects of Lizardian society is their connection to their home planet, Lizard-953-E. The planet is revered as a sacred place, and its high gravity, unique atmosphere, and rich resources have shaped the Lizardian way of life. The planet is home to sprawling cities that resemble modern Florida suburbs, each featuring spaceports, automated factories, and vast cultural hubs. The cities are governed by local councils, but ultimate authority rests with the Supreme Leader.

Lizardian art, music, and literature reflect their harsh and unyielding worldview. Their works often depict themes of power, conquest, and the inevitability of survival. However, despite their militaristic nature, the Lizards also have a deep appreciation for knowledge, with vast libraries and research centers dedicated to the pursuit of science and technology.

## History
The history of the Lizardian Empire begins long before their rise to interstellar power. The Lizards evolved on Lizard-953-E, a planet with an atmosphere rich in helium, oxygen, and xenon, and a surface marked by vast jungles, dense forests, and towering mountain ranges. This harsh environment shaped the Lizards into a resilient and resourceful species, known for their cunning intellect, complex social structures, and physical prowess. Their culture emphasizes efficiency, pragmatism, and survival at all costs, qualities that would later manifest in their expansionist tendencies.

Over thousands of years, the Lizards developed advanced technology, eventually harnessing the power of antimatter and manipulating space-time itself. They built massive Dyson spheres, constructed starships capable of traveling at near-light speeds, and developed cutting-edge weaponry that could obliterate entire civilizations. The Lizards' mastery of the forces of nature allowed them to terraform planets, creating entire systems capable of sustaining their growing empire.

Their expansion began with the unification of Lizard-953-E and its surrounding moons, followed by the conquest and integration of other planets, such as Lizard-616 A and B. The Lizards' military, bolstered by an ever-expanding fleet of powerful ships, drones, and automated weapons, ensured that their borders were protected from external threats.

## Political Structure and Leadership
The Lizardian Empire is led by a single ruler, known as the Supreme Leader, who is considered the pinnacle of wisdom, authority, and strength. The Supreme Leader is typically a member of the Lizardian aristocracy, a class of highly skilled and influential individuals who have risen to power through merit, political maneuvering, or military conquest. However, their reign is not without contention. The empire's leadership has often been a battleground of competing factions, each striving for control and dominance.

The Supreme Leader is supported by a vast network of officials, generals, and advisors, each responsible for managing different aspects of the empire, from its military operations to its scientific advancements. The Lizardian military is an integral part of the empire's political structure, with commanders and strategists holding significant sway over decision-making. The empire's military is led by a council of top generals who oversee the vast armies of Disassembly Drones, starships, and planetary defense systems.

In the past, the Lizardian Empire has faced internal strife. Factions within the aristocracy have vied for control, leading to brief periods of instability. However, the empire's military might and the cunning of its leaders have allowed it to maintain its dominance. The Lizards' political system is characterized by a strict hierarchy, where loyalty to the Supreme Leader and the empire is paramount.

## The Elkska Galaxy: The Lizardian Microgalaxy
The **Elkska Galaxy** is not just another galaxy; it represents the Lizards' most ambitious project to date—a self-sustaining, purposefully

constructed microcosm of the Lizardian Empire, designed to outlast the eventual decay of the universe. While the universe itself is headed toward the **Heat Death**, a state of maximum entropy where all thermodynamic processes cease, the Elkska Galaxy is being shaped as a sanctuary—a place where the Lizards can preserve their civilization and thrive well into the distant future.

### Creation and Purpose
The concept of a Microgalaxy was born out of the Lizards' understanding of astrophysics, especially the theory of **cosmic strings** and the nature of the universe's eventual collapse. Aware that the expansion of the universe is accelerating, with galaxies moving away from each other and the cosmic background radiation slowly diminishing, the Lizards realized that they could not wait idly for these cosmic forces to obliterate their star systems and the very fabric of their existence.

The solution? **Construct a new galaxy**—one that would be protected and self-sustaining. The Elkska Galaxy was conceived to be a **compact, tightly-bound galaxy** containing a network of interlinked star systems, each carefully selected and engineered to work in harmony with the others. By harnessing their immense technological capabilities, the Lizards began building an artificial galaxy that would survive the universe's eventual demise.

### Composition and Structure
The Elkska Galaxy is composed of **three satellite galaxies**, each designed to provide backup and redundancy in case of disasters or system failures. These satellite galaxies are smaller in size but contribute to the overall stability of the Microgalaxy, ensuring that the Lizards have multiple systems to rely on should any one fail.

- **Main Galaxy**: The central hub of the Elkska Galaxy. It contains the primary systems and planets where the Lizards' civilization has flourished. These star systems have been carefully chosen for their potential to be terraformed, stabilized, and harnessed for the needs of the Lizardian people.
- **Satellite Galaxies**: Three smaller galaxies, each containing several habitable planets, specialized industrial systems, and military outposts. These are designed to complement the central galaxy and provide backup in case of catastrophic failure in the primary systems. The satellites also serve as research centers for the Lizards, where their most advanced technologies are tested and refined.

The systems within the Elkska Galaxy are arranged in a precise configuration, ensuring that they are able to interact seamlessly. The galaxy is held together by advanced **gravitational manipulation technologies** that the Lizards have mastered, allowing them to control the gravitational forces between the star systems and maintain the integrity of the galaxy. This manipulation prevents any star systems from drifting apart and ensures that they remain within a controlled orbit.

### Technological Innovations
The Elkska Galaxy is a marvel of Lizardian engineering. Among the technological advancements that have made its creation possible are:

- **Stellar Engines**: The Lizards have deployed massive, galaxy-spanning stellar engines to control the movements of stars within the Elkska Galaxy. These engines ensure that the stars remain at optimal distances from one another, providing just the right amount of light, heat, and radiation for the planets within the galaxy to remain habitable.
- **Cosmic String Manipulation**: The Lizards have mastered the art of manipulating **cosmic strings**, which are theoretical one-dimensional structures that can stretch across vast distances of space. By bending and warping the cosmic strings within the Elkska Galaxy, the Lizards can create stable, closed systems that are impervious to the cosmic expansion happening outside of the galaxy. This allows the Elkska Galaxy to remain protected from the entropy of the universe.
- **Antimatter Power Plants**: The energy requirements of a galaxy as complex and vast as Elkska are immense. To meet these needs, the Lizards have harnessed the power of antimatter reactors, which provide a nearly unlimited source of clean, efficient energy. These reactors are scattered across the galaxy, ensuring that every system has a sufficient energy supply for both civilian and military needs.
- **Artificial Terraforming**: In addition to using natural resources, the Lizards have developed technology to artificially terraform planets. The Elkska Galaxy features numerous planets, each carefully engineered to meet the needs of Lizardian civilization. From lush, fertile worlds to harsh, resource-rich planets, the Elkska galaxy contains all the environments required for the Lizards' survival.
- **Immortal Cities**: The Lizards have also created **immortal cities**, technological marvels that use advanced nanotechnology and self-repair systems to remain operational for millennia. These cities are capable of sustaining their populations indefinitely without requiring traditional forms of maintenance or repair.

### The Role of the Lizards in Elkska
The Elkska Galaxy is not just a sanctuary for the Lizardian people—it is also a symbol of their dominance and foresight. The Lizards have designed every aspect of the galaxy to serve their needs and ensure their continued existence.

- **Scientific Research**: Elkska serves as a hub for the Lizards' most ambitious scientific research. The galaxy is home to numerous research stations that focus on fields ranging from advanced quantum mechanics to space-time manipulation. The Lizards are constantly striving to push the boundaries of what is possible, seeking new ways to expand their understanding of the universe and unlock the secrets of existence.
- **Military Dominance**: The Elkska Galaxy is also home to the most advanced military technologies the Lizards have ever developed. With fully autonomous fleets of starships, drones, and planetary defense systems, the Lizards are capable of defending their microgalaxy from any external threat. The sheer power of their military presence ensures that no outside force would dare to challenge the Lizards' reign.
- **Cultural Preservation**: The Lizards view Elkska as a means to preserve their cultural heritage and way of life. Within the galaxy, the Lizards continue to uphold their values of strength, dominance, and survival. Elkska provides them with the means to live in harmony with their environment, ensuring that their civilization can flourish for eons to come.

### The Future of the Lizardian Empire and Elkska
The Elkska Galaxy is just one part of the Lizards' larger goal: **to transcend the boundaries of their own universe** and potentially escape into another. Their ultimate ambition is to ensure the survival of their species far beyond the inevitable collapse of the universe. The Elkska Galaxy, with its technological marvels and artificial stability, is just the first step in a much grander plan.

### 4. Gravitational Influence and Tidal Forces
The Lizards' super-Earths like Lizard-953-E have stronger gravity than Earth. We can calculate the gravitational influence on satellites and nearby objects. For instance, tidal forces from Lizard-953-E on its moons would be significant.

**Tidal Forces Calculation:** The tidal force between two bodies (e.g., a planet and a moon) is given by:

$$
F_{\text{tidal}} = \frac{r^3}{2GMm}
$$

Where:

- G is the gravitational constant
- M is the mass of the planet
- m is the mass of the moon
- r is the distance between the two bodies

This can tell us the strength of tidal forces acting on moons like Scras or Mykavis orbiting B757N7.


### 5. Orbital Mechanics and Satellites
The Lizards have many moons in their systems. We can calculate the orbital period of satellites around their planets.

**Orbital Period of a Moon:** Using Kepler's Third Law, we can calculate the orbital period of moons around their planets:

$$
T^2 = \frac{4 \pi^2 r^3}{GM}
$$

Where:

- T is the orbital period
- r is the radius of the orbit (distance from planet center to moon)
- M is the mass of the planet
- G is the gravitational constant

### 6. Lizardian Economy and Resource Extraction
When extracting resources from planets or asteroids, the Lizards would need to calculate the volume and mass of the material being harvested. For example:

**Calculating Volume of an Asteroid:**

$$
V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3
$$

Where r is the radius of the asteroid. The mass would be:

$$
m = \rho \times V
$$

Where ρ is the density of the asteroid material.


### 7. Fleet and Military Logistics
If the Lizards are deploying a large fleet, we can calculate how many ships can be produced in a given time frame. If their shipyards produce x ships per day, the number of ships in a year would be:

$$
\text{Ships per Year} = x \times 365
$$

If each fleet consists of 100 ships, then the number of fleets produced in a year would be:

$$
\text{Fleets per Year} = 100x \times 365
$$
