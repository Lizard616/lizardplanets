Ambika is a temperate desert moon orbiting the exoplanet Lizard-599-N, located in the Lizard-599 star system. It is one of the seven major moons orbiting the planet. It is also the closest moon too the Lizard-599-N. It is the smallest of the three major moons orbiting Lizard-599-N, being smaller than Nguyet and Hag. Despite being a desert, the moon does have somewhat habitable conditions, but no life currently inhabits the moon.
