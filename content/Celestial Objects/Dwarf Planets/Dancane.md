{{planetaryoverview:Dancane}}

Dancane is the cosmic definition of "cold shoulder." Sitting pretty (and *frigidly*) in the No Belt, this 1,799 km-wide dwarf planet is basically the freezer aisle of the Lizard-953 system. Its surface is so cold that it practically screams, "Do not touch!"—not that anyone would dare.

Covered in layers of frost and jagged ice, Dancane looks like someone threw a snowball in space and forgot to melt it. But don't be fooled by its glacial demeanor—beneath all that ice lies an iron heart, because even the coldest exodwarfplanet has a soft side.

Its single moon, Anar, doesn't make things any warmer. Instead, Anar just hangs out at a safe distance, like an unimpressed roommate who doesn't want to share the heating bill. Together, they form the ultimate icy duo, silently judging the rest of the No Belt for not being frosty enough.

Dancane's slow spin (88-hour days!) and its chilly attitude make it the perfect place for anyone who loves the cold—or just really hates the idea of warmth. Pack a parka, though; it's colder than your ex's text replies out there.
