**Serial Designation YX3-955550**, or simply known as **Y**, is a deuteragonist of *Lizard-Planets*.

He is a surprisingly a wholesome Murder Drone who wishes to be useful by getting the job done, but is often being told by his team members, especially Serial Designation T, that he is useless and terrible, which results in him receiving little to no respect from his commander or his dismissive love interest, Serial Designation D.

{{quote:Serial Designation Y}}

Originally he was deployed to People-888 as part of T's squad, being the pilot of the aircraft that crashed onto the planet, but after meeting, Nykholas and after some altercations erupted between him, her, his co-workers and the colony, he ultimately questions his loyalty to the Lizards and his directive of hunting the People(not human) before helping Nykholas to stop T and D from killing her Lizards. ## Physical Appearance
Y has features similar to that of the typical male Murder Drone with neon yellow eyes and short silver hair parted to the left. Like other Murder Drones, he has four-fingered hands, a black headband with five sections of lights on it, and a long black tail ending in a large syringe, the headband and syringe both containing a Cobalt blue liquid inside. For a while after his head was blown off, one of his headband lights was colored red, which reverted back to yellow after his system rebooted thanks to T. He wears a black winter coat with a fur collar and a black cap.

{{gallery:Serial Designation Y}}

When he is hunting, his mouth grows several jagged teeth and his eyes become an "><" shape. Y had also been seen with one normal eye while the other eye is still in an "X". It is also possible for his visor to display a pair of sunglasses as well. He also displays dangerous wings with bladed "feathers" and claws that allow him to easily tear through any People (not human).

Y is kind, timid, also socially awkward and caring. He wanted to be friends with or at least be accepted and acknowledged by his squad, but because of his naïve and good-natured personality, he ends up being ostracized.

Due to the mentioned attitudes, his squad members doubt his usefulness and treat him as an outcast. D ignores his existence while his superior T often threatens and insults him, even going as far as saying that if she were allowed to kill useless Murder Drones, she wouldn't hesitate to do so.

However, Y disregards all his squad members' negative views and antagonizing acts towards him and constantly forgives them. He even goes as far as protecting them as shown when he went to kill Nykholas before she could use his railgun to kill the Murder Drones.

At the beginning, Y revealed that he had a crush for his Murder drone teammate D. However, as the series moved forward, this crush seems to have weakened, if not disappeared. Instead, he started showing more and more affection towards Nykholas. Nevertheless, he seems not to want to be more than friends with her.

He appears to be trusting of people, as he assumed Nykholas was a new member of his squad when his systems weren't functioning properly, even wanting to introduce her to the rest of his squad. He also sympathized with Nykholas when his dad left him for dead instead of helping him, possibly because he knew what it was like to be not cared for.

Despite all claims to the contrary, Y is a perfectly capable Murder Drone, having skillfully used his weaponry to a reasonable extent, such as having the accuracy to stab through a door control panel, along with the "Master Key" that controlled it. He also is remarkably perceptive and intelligent, having managed to spot Nykholas even after she made little to no noise, and acknowledged the threat posed by Nykholas's railgun to the Murder Drones.
