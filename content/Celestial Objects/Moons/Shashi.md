**Shashi** (also known as **Lizard-599-N I**) is one of seven of Lizard-599-N's natural satellite and the 5th largest moon in the Lizard-599 System.

## Tidal Effects
Due to the Lizard-599 rotating on its axis about twenty-eight times faster than Shashi makes one orbit around Lizard-599-N itself, this causes the gravitational pull of Lizard-599-N and Shashi to become "dragged", therefore causing surface ice of Lizard-599-N to bulge slightly.
