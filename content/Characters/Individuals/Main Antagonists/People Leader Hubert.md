## Physical Appearance

Hubert is an obese and grotesque figure, his bloated form a testament to his indulgent and gluttonous lifestyle. His pale, waxy skin appears almost translucent, stretched tightly over layers of fat, with sweat constantly beading on his brow. His face is round and heavily jowled, with sharp, beady eyes that glint with malice. His thinning hair is slicked back in a poor attempt at grooming, and his mouth is often twisted into a smug, condescending sneer. Hubert's short limbs seem disproportionately small compared to his massive torso, making his movements slow and laborious. Despite his physical limitations, his commanding presence is undeniable, amplified by his booming voice and the aura of dread he exudes.

## Attire

Hubert wears an ornate military uniform heavily adorned with medals and insignias, many of which are self-awarded. The outfit is tailored to his frame but strains at the seams, emphasizing his excessive size. A crimson sash draped across his chest and a gold-trimmed cape trailing behind him give him an air of grandeur that borders on parody.

## Personality

Hubert is a megalomaniac, driven by an insatiable hunger for power and control. He is cruel, manipulative, and devoid of empathy, viewing all other species as inferior and expendable. His arrogance is matched only by his cowardice; while he delights in issuing brutal orders, he avoids personal confrontation at all costs. Hubert is a master of propaganda, portraying himself as a savior to his people while masking his tyrannical actions and the suffering he inflicts.

## Motivations

- **Galaxy Domination:** Hubert envisions himself as the ruler of a vast interstellar empire, unchallenged by any civilization.
- **Hatred for the Lizards:** Hubert views the Lizards as a direct threat to his ambitions due to their technological superiority and communal values, which he sees as a challenge to his authoritarian rule.
- **Desire for Resources:** His aggression toward the Lizards is partly motivated by greed, as he covets their rich planets and advanced technology.

## Role in the 8/8/8888 Attack

Hubert orchestrated the attack with cold calculation, intending to cripple the Lizard civilization and establish dominance. The nuclear strike on the capital and the subsequent invasion were designed to sow fear and chaos, showcasing his ruthlessness. Despite his initial triumph, Hubert's plan ultimately backfired as the Lizards rallied against him with unprecedented fury.

## Downfall

Captured on 9/23/8921, Hubert was brought to face justice for his crimes. Refusing to accept accountability, he took his own life in a final act of cowardice, robbing the Lizards of the chance to hold him accountable for the atrocities he committed.

## Legacy

Hubert is remembered as one of the most reviled figures in Lizardian history, his name synonymous with destruction and betrayal. The Lizards' war with the People continues to be fueled by the scars he left behind, and his actions serve as a cautionary tale of unchecked ambition and cruelty.
