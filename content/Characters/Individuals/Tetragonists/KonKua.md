The origin of the artificial intelligence that would come to be known as the KonKua is made by [The Lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/). Either way, the program manifested in Drones and used Drones to destroy Planets and most of the Lifeforms living on the planet.

From there, it would spread to numerous [lizard](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/)-inhabited exoplanets, infecting more drones to find new hosts. Its power only grew when the Lizards of [Lizard-953-E](/celestial-objects/planets/super-earths/lizard-953-e/) allowed certain drones to be infected by the KonKua so they could better understand it. Drones like Obli were deliberately infected.

Fortunately, the KonKua succeeded in finding a new host.

Hosts of the KonKua become subject to various mechanical and organic mutations affecting their bodies. These mutations can result in very surreal and unnatural effects in the infected drone, such as being able to reconstruct themselves, unintentionally converting mechanical material into organic substances, creating and grafting techno-organic parts, and even allowing certain drones to cheat death in ways that should be impossible.

The severity of an inherited infection may vary depending on the parent's condition.

It appears that those infected by the KonKua will develop a special techno-organic heart-like organ, which, as long as it is not destroyed permanently, can enable the drone to eventually reconstruct itself,

## Abilities
| **Ability** | **Function/Example** |
|-------------|----------------------|
| **KonKua Possession** | The KonKua's most noteworthy ability is the complete possession of a host. |
| **Matter Transformation** | KonKua can alter the fundamental structure of matter, transforming substances into entirely different forms or compositions. This ability could be used for transmutation, weapon creation, or altering environments on a Sub-atomic level. The KonKua can Transform objects into a higher dimension. |
| **Telekinesis** | KonKua can move objects with its mind, from the smallest particles to massive celestial bodies. This power extends to manipulating the fundamental forces in space, allowing it to control the environment around it with incredible precision. |
| **Teleportation** | KonKua can teleport instantly to any location in space, bypassing all physical barriers. This makes it an elusive and unpredictable force, able to appear and disappear at will. |
| **Illusion Creation** | Whenever using its powers, the KonKua can conjure lines of code, which vary in color depending on the host. It is unknown if this is controllable or not. |
| **Shapeshifting** | While in control of one, the KonKua can alter the appearance of their host. For example, your right hand can be replaced with a set of claws; you also sprouted wings. you can also grow a tail and wings. |
| **Black Hole manipulation** | KonKua can create, control, and manipulate black holes, which are extremely dense regions of spacetime with gravitational pulls so strong that not even light can escape. KonKua can use black holes as weapons, traps, or means of transportation. |
| **Invisibility/Intangibility** | KonKua's Hosts can become intangible at will, allowing it to pass through solid objects without being affected by physical boundaries. This power makes it nearly invulnerable to attacks that rely on physical or material forces. |
| **Regeneration** | Its hosts can regenerate themselves easily by drawing the appropriate nearby matter. This process is sped up when the KonKua is in full possession, recreating severed limbs and heads in mere milliseconds. |
| **Handiness** | The KonKua appears to have demonstrated an aptitude for masterful maneuvering with its various kinds of limbs (tentacles, claws, hands) while operating its hosts. |
| **False Vacuum Manipulation (Vacuum Decay)** | KonKua can influence the vacuum of space itself, inducing a state known as false vacuum decay. This could theoretically alter the fundamental laws of physics, leading to the collapse of the universe's structure and potentially even destroying it. |
| **Spacetime Warping** | KonKua can warp spacetime itself, altering the fabric of reality. This could involve creating anomalies in the space-time continuum, bending light, causing time dilation, or creating shortcuts through space for travel. |
| **Virus Blast** | KonKua's Hosts can emit blasts that act like viruses, potentially attacking systems or entities by corrupting their internal functions or causing rapid decay. This could be used to weaken or disable opponents, even targeting their technological systems. |
| **Gravastar Manipulation** | Similar to black holes, KonKua can manipulate gravastars, hypothetical objects that are stable and do not collapse into singularities. Gravastars may offer KonKua further control over spacetime and energy. |
| **Star Manipulation** | KonKua can control stars, using telekinesis to alter their paths, mass, and behavior. It can manipulate stellar energy and matter, potentially causing supernovae, black holes, or even extinguishing stars. |
| **Antimatter Manipulation** | KonKua has the ability to manipulate antimatter, the counterpart to matter, which when brought into contact with regular matter results in an explosive annihilation. This gives KonKua control over one of the most destructive forces in the universe. |
| **Cosmic String Manipulation** | KonKua can control and manipulate cosmic strings, which are hypothetical objects that stretch across the universe. These strings are theorized to have extreme gravitational effects and could influence the very structure of spacetime. KonKua can use these strings for various purposes, including manipulating space itself. |

## Weaknesses
The Konkua has no known weaknesses.

## Trivia
- When manifested by Lizardina Drones, the KonKua seems to be hostile to smooth and reflective surfaces by cracking them violently.
- Due to being an intelligence that possesses hosts, as well as other machinery such as claws/tentacles, the KonKua is never shown in a definitive form. Its consciousness, however, takes the form of a black hole or False Vacuum.
