The **Phinno** are a small, sapient humanoid species native to the glaciated exoplanet **Lizard-635-B**, though genetic studies strongly suggest they are not indigenous to this world. Their physiology points toward a panspermia origin, likely evolving on **Yukippe** — a lush, warm moon orbiting a neighboring gas giant Lizard-635-N, before natural dispersal or interplanetary drift seeded them onto Lizard-635-B eons ago.

Standing between **1.15 and 1.25 meters**, the Phinno possess a compact, agile frame optimized for rapid, precise motion. Their musculature is dense with highly elastic fibers, enabling feats of explosive acceleration despite their small size. Their **cranial structure** is marked by feline-like ears adapted for directional hearing, while their **long, whip-like tail** serves as a balance organ and, occasionally, a close-quarters combat appendage.

Their skin is a pale, translucent shade with a faint frost-like shimmer, due to **subdermal crystalline nano-structures** that refract ambient light and assist in environmental camouflage. These structures serve as natural thermoregulators, allowing the Phinno to endure temperatures that would freeze most organic life solid.

Perhaps their most astonishing biological trait is their capacity for **bio-static stasis** — a conscious, voluntary shutdown of visible movement and metabolism, allowing them to remain perfectly immobile when observed. This phenomenon, driven by specialized neural control over micro-muscle fibers, renders them near-invisible to both organic predators and technological sensors when stationary.

### Neurotoxic Enzyme & Hypothalamic Attack
The Phinno's method of predation — and warfare — revolves around their **precision cranial bite**. Hidden within their jaw are short, retractable fangs capable of penetrating bone with surgical accuracy. Upon biting, they deliver **Hypothermolysin-A**, a specialized enzyme that chemically disables the victim's **hypothalamus**, the brain's regulatory center for autonomic functions like temperature control, heart rate, and homeostasis.

This enzyme triggers:

- **Autonomic shutdown** within seconds
- **Cardiac arrest and thermoregulatory collapse**
- **Rapid systemic hypothermia**, often fatal within a minute

This mode of attack was likely a natural predatory adaptation on **Yukippe**, used against large cold-blooded fauna in sub-zero environments. On the battlefield, it functions as an immediate incapacitation method, with most victims unable to react or raise an alarm.

### Origins & Panspermia Hypothesis
While long considered natives of **Lizard-635-B**, Phinno genetic sequences contain distinct markers aligned with extremophile organisms found on **Yukippe**, a frozen moon rich in simple organic compounds and subsurface liquid oceans. Evidence suggests early Phinno ancestors drifted to Lizard-635-B aboard interplanetary ejecta — a rare but plausible case of **natural panspermia** — where they evolved into the sentient, lethal beings they are today.

This theory is supported by isotopic analysis of their bone structure, which contains trace elements more abundant on Yukippe than on Lizard-635-B, and by fossil records showing sudden genetic divergence on Lizard-635-B roughly **2.1 million years ago**.

### Cultural Evolution & the People's War
For most of their recorded history, the Phinno maintained a minimalist, non-expansionist society. They constructed intricate ice-bound cities using advanced manipulation of frozen carbon composites, emphasizing harmony with their environment and a deep cultural respect for silence, precision, and patience.

That peace ended when the **People** — a galactic species notorious for resource exploitation — arrived and began systematically destroying Phinno settlements, poisoning glacial rivers, and mining ancient ice structures for industrial profit.

Rather than face extinction, the Phinno reached out to the only power capable of standing against the People: the **Lizards of the Lizardian Empire**.

### Affiliations
- **Primary Alliance:** **Lizardian Empire**
  - The Phinno serve as covert operatives, assassins, and special reconnaissance agents within the Lizardian Allied Forces.
  - Their combat doctrine complements the Lizards' technological superiority with biological precision strikes and fear tactics.
  - Phinno commandos are often deployed for surgical eliminations, deep infiltration, and psychological operations behind enemy lines.
- **Secondary Allies:**
  - **Disassembly Drones** — Sharing operational fields with Lizardian commando units, Phinno occasionally work alongside the Drones, especially in high-risk suppression and extermination missions.
  - **Sympathizer Cells** in fringe systems that oppose the People's expansion often assist in Phinno covert operations, seeing them as a symbol of resistance.
- **Declared Enemies:**
  - **The People** — Considered enemies of Phinno survival and freedom. The Phinno's ongoing silent war against the People is one of annihilation rather than deterrence.
  - **Corporate Mining Guilds of the Outer Rim** — Many have faced targeted strikes and disappearances traced back to Phinno operatives when caught violating planetary sanctity.


### Combat Doctrine & Behavior
The Phinno are guided by a strict combat ethos:

- **Absolute Silence** — They do not speak in combat. Every movement is calculated for maximum impact.
- **Fatal Precision** — They attack only when a guaranteed kill is within reach. Anything less is dishonorable and inefficient.
- **Psychological Warfare** — Phinno exploit fear, rumors, and enemy superstition. The “unmoving cloaked figure” is a terror legend among enemy soldiers.

When confronted, Phinno will enter **bio-static freeze**, making them blend into the background like a living statue. Once visual contact is broken, they can close a gap within fractions of a second, delivering a cranial bite and disappearing before alarms can sound.

### Philosophy, Symbolism & The Black Robe
The black robe worn by all Phinno is more than mere attire — it is a declaration of mourning and resistance. Each robe is handcrafted from native polymer-fibers and bears no insignia. The Phinno believe that silence is the ultimate statement, and the robe is a visual representation of their silent war against exploitation and environmental ruin.

A Phinno's presence on the battlefield is never announced. They leave no symbols, no signatures, and rarely survivors. To them, every successful mission is a silent echo that will haunt their enemies forever.
