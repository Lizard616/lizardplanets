Beryl Nuts can be collected from Beryl Nut Bushes found on Planet Lizard-597-B by approaching them and Plucking them of. Each bush yields five Nuts, and regrows after three Solar days from the point it was harvested, provided the bush hasn't been cut by a Lizard or run over with a Starship.
