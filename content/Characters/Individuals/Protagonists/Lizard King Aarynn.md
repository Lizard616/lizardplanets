Aarynn is a Big male Lizard who has sticky Acidic blood

{{gallery:Lizard King Aarynn}}

Neither the date of incorporation nor the founders of the Sector are fully known. Aarynn is a huge, interstellar private Sector possibly stationed on Lizard-357-B whose main services are manufacturing various things, ranging from branded pens and civilian items to autonomous robots called "drones," Like military (Murder Drones) varieties.

Aarynn has a considerable amount of free resources and is able to put together many things quickly, almost without pause. It is noted that the Sector's products and services are available in almost every corner of the universe, regardless of how far away the planet or civilizations are.

The Sector also specializes in exoplanet excavation, mainly assisting Lizards with exoplanet resource extraction. One of them was People-888, where Aarynn. inc offered assistance to Lizards by sending in needed equipment and huge amounts of Slaves who were used just like Servants.

## Aarynn. inc-Lizity's People-888 Collaboration
Despite the People (not human)'s efficiency in their work, they were mistreated and disregarded for their sentience, akin to being slaves. After the People-888 core was accidentally melted and destroyed, all biological life on the exoplanet went extinct. Shortly after the People-888 crisis, the now-abandoned People (not humans) quickly moved on to create their future for themselves.

Back on Lizard-967-X7-Kokushibo, Aarynn didn't like the idea of sentient, rebellious beings, so they soon ushered in several launch pods containing a dangerous weapon - squads of Drones, known as "Murder Drones" by their prey, the People (not humans). They were sent on People-888 with only one directive: kill any and all People (not humans) they find. However, the Sector intentionally left a failsafe within the Murder drones Drones, an under-par cooling system, making them need to consume Blood from the People (not humans) to keep themselves from overheating. This fatal and broken system was implemented so that the Murder Drones will stick to their directive and self-destruct once they complete their mission, making them easily disposed of without the need for manually removing them.

## Aarynn. inc Intervention
After the destruction of Serial Designation T, the Sector did not fully abandon its goals and decided to intervene in a further developing crisis. At first, alongside the main known squad of Murder Drones, another 3 launch pods containing 3 other squads were deployed on the planet. After yet another destruction and a now-modified form of T - Eldritch T, the Sector decided to fully reconstruct the destroyed Drone, and alongside her, deploy a Aarynn. inc Certified Technician Lizard - Kristine, whose plans for the Rogue People (not human) Colony are still unknown.
