The Xanak Palm (also known as Tropi Palm) is a plant species Native to Lizard-967-X7-Kokushibo. And is Believed to carry the Ancestors of The Lizard Species. and also has sacred Fruit called Ozeadine. The fruit is Very bioluminescent and sweet. The lizards Farm the palms for there sweet and healthy fruit. These palms live in the warm and tropical regions of Planet Lizard-967-X7-Kokushibo.
