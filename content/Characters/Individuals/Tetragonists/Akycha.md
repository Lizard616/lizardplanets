{{character:Akycha}}

She was a feral Murder Drone who lived down in the depths of Lizard-5753, in an underground office facility where she hid from the **Anti-Drone Sentinels** and scavenged body parts from their victims and adding them to herself. She managed to capture Nykolas Jecnob, Kristine , D and Y when they went to chase after Ruqen who had stolen the "key" to the secret elevator.

However, her attempts to salvage parts from Nykolas

and have the Sentinels close in on Y and a wounded Kristine were foiled by Hudd temporarily manifesting through Nykolas and using the K̶̨̤̤͉̳̆̈́̀͒͝ð̸̫͚̲̻͙͋̋͊͒̚ņ̴̜͎̜̟̃̋̅̏̏̕k̵̻̝͖̠̻̒͂́͐͒µ̷͕͉̳̺̫͂̈́̅̚͝å̷̦̬̱̰͛̈́̑̈́̽͜ to unlock the doors that kept the Sentinels out of her safety zone.

Ultimately, she was killed when she failed to close the door in time and her head was crushed underfoot by one of the Sentinels.

## Appearance
Akycha has neon orange eyes and dark reddish hair tied up into a ponytail. She also dons antlers that go over her hard hat, containing various items likely scavenged throughout the facility. She has a long, thin black tail with the tip of it curling around the handle of a kitchen knife.
