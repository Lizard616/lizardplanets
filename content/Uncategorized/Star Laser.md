Star Laser as a weapon where the satellites of a Dyson swarm surrounding a sun act as a phased array laser emitter capable of delivering their energy to a planet-sized target at a range of millions of light years.

{{gallery:Star Laser}}

The laser could do anything from wiping out the atmosphere on the target planet to cracking the planet in half depending on the focus of the beam (a collimated beam would be the diameter of the Dyson sphere used to generate it, whereas a focused beam could be more concentrated). Other factors that could effect the beam's intensity include the size and energy output of the sun surrounded by the Dyson Swarm, the efficiency of the swarm's solar energy conversion, and the amount of time the laser was pointed at the target planet.The Lizards, an extraterrestrial race, are renowned for their ingenuity and advanced technological prowess. Hailing from the distant star system of Lizard-967, the Lizards have constructed a colossal Dyson Sphere known as Solarium. This artificial megastructure encapsulates their entire star, harnessing its energy on an unprecedented scale.

One of the most awe-inspiring features of the Lizards' Dyson Sphere is the Solar Beam Nexus, a colossal laser system integrated into the structure. This powerful weapon serves both defensive and offensive purposes, showcasing the Lizards' commitment to protecting their civilization and interests.

The Dyson Sphere's Solar Beam Nexus is composed of an intricate network of energy conduits and amplifiers that channel the star's immense power into a focused laser beam. This beam can be precisely directed towards potential threats or celestial targets, demonstrating the Lizards' mastery over harnessing stellar energy for strategic purposes.

The Solar Beam Nexus has become a symbol of the Lizards' technological dominance and deterrence in the vast expanse of Lizarian space. While primarily designed for defense, the Lizards' laser system is also capable of precision strikes when necessary, making it a formidable tool for maintaining order and safeguarding the interests of the Lizardian civilization across the cosmos.
