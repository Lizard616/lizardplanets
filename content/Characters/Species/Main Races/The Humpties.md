{{droneinfo:Humpties}}

The Humpties are a highly peculiar alien race, known for their immense size and notable obesity, which is a defining characteristic of their species. Their physical appearance consists of large, spherical bodies with multiple short, stubby limbs and small heads with beady eyes. They are covered in a thick, leathery skin that ranges from a dull green to a mottled purple hue, and they have an insatiable appetite for resources, often driven by their need to sustain their massive forms.

The Humpties are deeply prideful and believe in the supremacy of their race, viewing their size as a sign of strength and dominance. Despite their size, they are not particularly agile, relying instead on their immense endurance and capacity for destruction. Their societies are organized around massive, fortress-like cities, which are heavily fortified due to their tendency to overconsume and pollute their environment. They are technologically advanced in certain areas, such as resource extraction and weaponry, though their technology often has a clunky and inefficient feel due to their cumbersome nature.

In their conflict with the Lizards, the Humpties are driven by their insatiable hunger for more resources, which leads them to attempt to conquer planets like Lizard-953-E. They believe that the rich resources of the Lizard homeworld are essential for their survival, as they continue to expand and maintain their gluttonous empire. The Lizards, on the other hand, see the Humpties as a threat not only to their resources but also to the delicate balance of their ecosystem and military dominance.

The Humpties' arrogance and disregard for the well-being of other species lead them to clash with the Lizards, who view their constant resource consumption and pollution as a dangerous and reckless path. The Lizards' advanced technology, combined with their efficient military strategies, has proven to be a formidable challenge for the Humpties, who have suffered heavy losses in their attempts to invade Lizard-953-E.

Their conflict with the Lizards is ongoing, with the Humpties refusing to back down despite the overwhelming opposition. Their motivations remain tied to their gluttonous nature and desire to expand their empire at any cost, making them a persistent threat in the universe.
