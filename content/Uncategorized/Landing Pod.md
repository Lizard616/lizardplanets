As the name suggests, Landing Pods are spaceships used to land on different planets. They were used by the lizards to get to other planets. One landing pod was also temporarily used by Arynn as a base when Tolken banished himself from the Etakani nebula.

## Appearance
Landing pods have four legs used to land and stick onto walls like a spider or crab. These legs most likely absorb shock from a fall from high areas, similar to the knees of a lizard. It has 4 small windows, designed to withstand pressures that can crush a car in seconds, many screens, a high-tech communication system, and a control panel inside.
