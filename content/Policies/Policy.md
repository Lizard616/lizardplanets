Policies and guidelines have been developed on **Lizard-Planets Wiki** to further our goal of creating a freely editable **Lizard-Planets** database.

Policies have community consensus and apply to all editors. **Lizard-Planets Wiki** strives to create a welcoming and fun environment for those who are civil to others, assume good faith, seek consensus in discussions, and work towards the goal of creating an increasingly comprehensive database about everything in the **Lizard-Planets** universe.

Policies need to be approached with common sense; adhere to the spirit rather than the letter of the rules, and be prepared to ignore the rules on the rare occasions when they conflict with the goal of improving the wiki.

## Where do our policies come from?
Generally, Lizard-Planets Wiki policies come from one of three sources:

- From a standard or good practice that has been followed subconsciously for some time because it is common sense.
- From a deliberate proposal by a user or a group of users.
- From other great wikis that use these policies.
