Lizard-635-B is a frigid, glaciated exoplanet located in a stable orbit within the Lizard-635 star system. Its surface is dominated by vast ice sheets, towering glaciers, and jagged frozen landscapes, punctuated by pockets of geothermal activity that provide rare oases of warmth. The planet's atmosphere is thin but breathable, with trace gases adapted to preserve its frigid climate.

Though the Phinno have long been considered native to Lizard-635-B, extensive genetic and isotopic analyses reveal a more complex origin story. Their unique biology suggests a panspermia heritage, having likely evolved on Yukippe — a neighboring rocky, frozen moon orbiting a massive gas giant in the same star system. Natural dispersal, possibly via interplanetary ejecta, seeded Phinno ancestors onto Lizard-635-B eons ago, where they adapted to its extreme conditions and flourished.

The planet's environment demands remarkable resilience: temperatures plunge far below freezing for most of the year, but the Phinno thrive here thanks to subdermal crystalline nanostructures that refract ambient light and regulate body temperature, granting them near-imperceptible camouflage and protection against the cold. Lizard-635-B's ice-bound cities, constructed from frozen carbon composites and sustained by geothermal vents, showcase the Phinno's advanced environmental engineering and cultural harmony with their harsh world.

Lizard-635-B's ecological niches, shaped by the planet's geologic youth and ice dynamics, host a fragile but complex biosphere. The Phinno, standing between 1.15 and 1.25 meters tall with agile frames, navigate this world with grace, their feline-like ears and whip-like tails adapted to silent, precise movement.

The planet also serves as the backdrop for the Phinno's long-standing struggle against the People — a resource-hungry galactic species whose exploitation threatens Lizard-635-B's fragile ecosystems. In response, the Phinno allied with the technologically superior Lizardian Empire, becoming stealth operatives and assassins renowned for their bio-static stasis ability and lethal precision.

Lizard-635-B is thus not only a world of ice and silence but also a crucible where survival, resistance, and ancient origins converge — a planet whose shadowy defenders embody the silent war waged beneath its frozen surface.
