Lizard-722-O is the code-name of the exoplanet It was a terrestrial exoplanet colonized by the [Lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/), and valued at least in part for Biological engineering, Pathogen research, and Vaccine manufacturing. The planet was the site of a devastating "Bio spill" which turned test subjects into ruthless beasts that roam the planet, cruel to all biological fauna. It is now the current home of the "rogue" zombies that were left behind in the wake of the biological extinction event, along with at least three known Exterminators sent by the [Lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) to exterminate all Biospill hazards.

## Before the Biospill
Lizard-722-O seemed to be a planet that could support both human and [Lizard](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) life. Prior to the Biospill, a quick glance revealed brown continents, red oceans of water, and clouds in the atmosphere, all of which point to an atmosphere resembling our own. Cities and other indications of Earth-like life were widespread, and it was also home to a large number of humans and [lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/).

## Biospill
A breach occurred during the [lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/)' efforts to contain the original "test subject," allowing the host to escape. But in the process, the host—which was immediately severed from the body—formed the virus. All test subjects were infected, though, when the virus fell into the Biospill pit beneath the containment area.

## After Biospill
Without a vaccine, Lizard-722-O plunged swiftly into a hostile forest planet, where the virus killed all of the [lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) and humans. The electrical grid and other [lizard](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/)-made infrastructure, however, survived, enabling the now-liberated "zombies" to rebuild. But the [lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) sent three exterminators to the planet, posing as the lizards on [Lizard-953-E](/celestial-objects/planets/super-earths/lizard-953-e/).

## Trivia
- There seems to be a moon orbiting around Lizard-722-O, and it has a enormous ring system that resembles Saturn's.
- The moon of Lizard-722-O seems to resemble the moon of our own Earth as well as the ringed moon.
- The planet might have been inhabited by a variety of human and [lizard](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) races rather than being assigned to just one.
- Lizard-722-O is confirmed to orbit its star at a slower speed than Earth does, meaning the days and/or months on Lizard-722-O don't exactly align with Earth months.

## Exterminators
#### Overview
The Exterminators are highly trained **[Lizard](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) operatives**, deployed to reclaim the infected planet and eliminate the rogue beasts spawned by the catastrophic Biospill. They are equipped with advanced biohazard protection and specialized weaponry, enabling them to survive the hostile environment and engage with mutated threats.

### Appearance
- **Gas Masks**:
  - Sleek, matte black masks designed to filter out airborne pathogens and toxic gases in the Virus-ridden atmosphere.
  - Equipped with glowing visors, typically red or green, for enhanced vision in the dark and dense mist of Lizard-722-O.
- **Armor**:
  - Reinforced, lightweight biohazard suits made of **carbon-alloy nanofiber** for protection against physical attacks and viral contamination.
  - The suits are segmented for mobility and fitted with **claw-resistant plating** on critical areas such as the chest and limbs.
  - Each suit features a distinctive emblem of the Lizardian Empire and glowing circuitry, symbolizing their connection to **KonKua** (the Lizardian AI overseeing their mission).
- **Weapons**:
- ***Plasma Rifles**: Compact and high-energy weapons designed to incinerate rogue beasts on contact.
- ***Anti-Virus Grenades**: Explosive devices that release a localized burst of pathogen-neutralizing agents, killing infected organisms within a radius.
- ***Flamethrowers**: For crowd control and clearing infected vegetation or rogue nests.
- ***Energy Blades**: Retractable melee weapons capable of cutting through infected hide with precision.
- **Additional Gear**:
  - Built-in **oxygen tanks** for prolonged operations in the dense, virus-laden atmosphere.
- ***Holo-maps** embedded in the armor for real-time navigation through the overgrown forests and abandoned ruins.

### Personality and Training
- **Training**:
**Exterminators undergo rigorous physical and psychological conditioning, preparing them for the horrors of rogue-infested zones.
**They are trained in **biological containment**, **close-quarters combat**, and **survival tactics** for extended missions in isolated environments.
- **Personality**:
  - **Exterminators** are disciplined and mission-focused, but their personalities may vary:
  - **Commander**: Tactical and calculating, often leading the charge.
  - **Specialist**: Expert in biohazard research, focused on studying the Virus for potential weaknesses.
  - **Heavy Combatant**: Brash and fearless, excelling in frontline combat with flamethrowers and energy blades.

### Mission and Tactics
- **Primary Objective**:  Eradicate all Biospill hazards, including rogue beasts, infected plants, and remnants of the Virus.
- **Tactics**:
- **Stealth**: Exterminators often operate in small, highly mobile squads to avoid detection by large packs of rogue beasts.
- **Cleanse and Contain**: They systematically neutralize infected zones, using anti-virus grenades to sanitize the area.
- **Combat**: Engage rogue beasts with precision weaponry, relying on flamethrowers and plasma rifles to handle larger swarms.
- **Challenges**:
  - The rogue beasts are highly resilient, with heightened senses and rapid reflexes, making them formidable opponents.
  - The dense forests and abandoned cities provide countless hiding spots for infected creatures, requiring meticulous scanning and coordination.

### Cultural Significance
- The Exterminators are viewed as heroes by the Lizardian Empire, their courage and skill celebrated in stories and songs.
- Their armor and weapons, adorned with glowing emblems, serve as symbols of hope and resilience for the Lizardian people.
