## Appearance
It looks like a whale from a distance, however, a closer look reveals a bird-like beak and head with small eyes, indicating that it may have poor eyesight. It has four very long legs, which it uses to move around and swim.

## Behavior
The Sea Elephant is a passive creature. It will not attack the People (not human) and will not run away when approached. If attacked by a People (not human), it will attempt to gallop away from the People (not human) before settling down again a short while after. It is bouncy and propels the People (not human) high up into the air when jumped on, even if dead.

Occasionally it will raise itself onto its hind legs and then drop back down.

{{gallery:Sea Elephant}}

Sea Elephants can be sometimes found swimming, but with no special Features attributed to it, making it look like they're just floating in the water. Their long legs help them to move in deep waters.

It makes low-pitched sounds when nearby, which sound similar to water-dwelling whales found on Earth.
