**Serial Designation T**, or simply **T**, is a formidable Murder drone with a sleek, metallic exoskeleton. Like other drones, T is equipped with a highly efficient, syringe-like tail, but his tail is filled with a unique corrosive substance—[Yabitanite](/uncategorized/yibitanite/)—which can melt through almost any material. This makes T both a deadly combatant and a highly effective tool in precision dismantling.

T's design is smooth and aerodynamic, sporting sharp-edged limbs and retractable blades in his arms for close-quarters combat. His optics glow with a sharp amber hue, giving him a calculating, almost predatory look when focused on a target. His exterior is reinforced for durability, but his movements remain fast and agile.

As a fellow drone of [Arynn](/characters/individuals/protagonists/lizard-king-aarynn/), the Lizard King, T is loyal to the cause of defending the [lizards](/characters/species/main-races/lizards/) and aiding [Arynn](/characters/individuals/protagonists/lizard-king-aarynn/) in his planet terraforming projects. He works well in a squad, providing essential firepower and support with his [Yabitanite](/uncategorized/yibitanite/) syringe tail, especially when dissolving obstacles or enemies.

T has a calm yet assertive demeanor, more focused on efficiency and precision, and he maintains a good rapport with the other drones, often acting as a methodical counterpart to the more energetic or aggressive members of the group.

## Physical Description & Personality
T has bushy white hair, similar to that of [Y](/characters/individuals/deuteragonists/serial-designation-y/)'s. He is significantly taller than the average male Murder Drone, but weighs a few pounds heavier. He wears a light-cyan fur sweater, with White armbands on his arms and has orange-and-black caution stripes, and he also wears a black beanie-like hat.. He has a relatively calm personality, and is calm-tempered with his squadmates, often chill with them. He is an incredibly efficient DD, using powerful strategies such as Plasma guns, EMP waves, and more to kill [People](/characters/species/main-races/people-not-humans/)'s as quickly as possible. He is also of course, rather destructive, sometimes resorting to nothing but explosives to kill. Serial Designation T's personality is shaped by his precision and calculated efficiency, with a dash of German stoicism. He is methodical in battle, often staying quiet and focused, preferring to let his actions speak louder than words. Despite his calculated demeanor, T's German speech gives him a unique charm; he is sometimes unintentionally humorous when translating idiomatic phrases. His sarcasm can be biting, but he is loyal and protective, especially toward Arynn. T can also display a touch of arrogance, particularly when deploying his corrosive Yabitanite, which he considers his deadliest weapon.
