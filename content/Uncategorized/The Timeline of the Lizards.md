| **Year** | **Civilization Stage** | **Technological/Scientific Achievements** | **Key Developments** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **2111** | **Emergence of the Lizards** | Evolution of a basic species, possibly reptilian-like, starting life on their homeworld. | Early tribes form; basic survival instincts, simple tools. |
| **3071** | **Discovery of Fire and Stone Tools** | Mastery over fire, beginning of toolmaking using stone, early forms of social organization. | Control of fire, use of stone tools like axes and knives. |
| **4436** | **Ancient Egyptian-Level Society** | Mastery over agriculture, early metallurgy (copper, bronze), large-scale construction, early writing. | Monumental architecture, writing systems, religious institutions. |
| **5503** | **Medieval Age** | Development of feudal systems, early mechanical devices, first firearms, and exploration. | Feudal governance, knights, and local lords. Start of early scientific and technological ideas. |
| **6645** | **Industrial Age** | Steam power, mechanized production, railroads, mass production, significant scientific breakthroughs. | Urbanization, factory systems, rise of capitalism, scientific advancements. |
| **7732** | **Type 1.5 Civilization** | Harnessing planetary resources, space exploration, the dawn of planetary engineering. | Planetary-scale engineering, limited interplanetary travel. |
| **7946** | **Type 2 Civilization** | Mastery over star systems, energy extraction from stars, large-scale space infrastructure. | Construction of Dyson spheres or similar structures, expansion within their star system. |
| **8034** | **Type 2.5 Civilization** | Advanced interstellar travel, early galactic energy manipulation, galactic-scale engineering. | Control over entire star systems, travel across multiple star systems. |
| **9537** | **Type 2.97 Civilization** | Near-total mastery of energy and space-time, control over a vast part of the galaxy. | Highly advanced AI, advanced weaponry, interstellar politics, vast galactic empire. |
| **9946** | **Type 3 Civilization** | Mastery over the entire galaxy, control over cosmic phenomena, potential manipulation of space-time. | Full galactic unity, manipulation of stars, black holes, and energy sources on a galactic scale. |
