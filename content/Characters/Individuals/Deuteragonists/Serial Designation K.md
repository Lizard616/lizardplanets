**K** is a humanoid drone with a striking and distinctive appearance, blending the sleek and deadly framework of a disassembly drone with uniquely masculine traits that set him apart from his counterparts.

## Head and Face
K's head features messy, snow-white "hair" that cascades in soft, chaotic layers, adding an expressive and almost rebellious personality to his mechanical form. Despite his female-coded disassembly drone body, his hair and overall demeanor give him a distinctly masculine aura. His face is dominated by a smooth, glossy visor that glows with piercing, neon blue slits for eyes. These glowing slits pulse subtly, exuding a sense of calculated intelligence and a hint of emotion. Perched atop his head is a pair of red-tinted goggles, giving him a functional yet stylish edge, as if he's ready for exploration or high-speed operations.

## Upper Body
K's torso maintains the signature sleekness of disassembly drones, with integrated white plating resembling a turtleneck sweater that smooths into his chassis. The design retains an elegant, streamlined form but also hints at the lethal capabilities hidden within. His shoulders are slightly rounded, and his overall frame is compact and nimble, optimized for efficiency and speed. The aesthetic of his upper body strikes a balance between industrial functionality and humanoid allure, with just enough detail to feel both menacing and approachable.

## Arms and Hands
K's arms are long and slender, ending in dexterous hands that are both functional and elegant. His forearms are adorned with faint glowing lines of blue energy, subtly pulsing as though reflecting his current energy levels or mood. Despite the sharp, mechanical nature of his arms, they are designed with grace and precision, capable of intricate tasks as well as combat. The hazard stripes on his limbs—black and yellow—add an industrial, warning-like motif, marking him as dangerous and highly specialized.

## Legs and Lower Body
K's legs mirror the angular and sleek design of a disassembly drone, built for agility and speed. They are primarily black with bold yellow hazard stripes encircling his thighs and calves, an aesthetic reminder of the danger he poses. The long, slightly exaggerated proportions of his legs make him appear taller and more imposing, yet their design speaks to a balance of elegance and lethality. These legs are clearly made for rapid traversal, leaping, and combat, seamlessly blending power with flexibility.

## Aura and Personality
Despite his female-shaped disassembly drone body, K exudes a male personality that is both confident and self-assured. His voice is deep but laced with a synthetic undertone, adding a unique charm to his robotic identity. He carries himself with an air of efficiency and competence, but his personality hints at a deeper layer of humor and individuality, making him stand out from other drones. K is the embodiment of versatility—a perfect blend of deadly precision and charismatic personality.

## Key Traits
- **Male-coded voice and mannerisms:** His voice is deep and synthetic, carrying an assertive tone that commands attention.
- **Stylish design:** The red goggles, hazard stripes, and glowing blue highlights add both character and functionality to his appearance.
- **Duality:** K's body is feminine in structure, but his personality, confidence, and attitude exude masculinity, creating an intriguing juxtaposition.
